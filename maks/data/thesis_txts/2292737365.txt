Abstract!
!
Calf! diarrhoea! is! a! disease! that! is! commonly! seen! in! dairy! calves! and! it! is! associated! with!
reduced!animal!welfare.!Furthermore!it!reduces!the!performance!of!affected!animals!and!can!
cause!substantial!economic!losses!to!farmers.!
This!study!was!performed!a!clinical!field!trial!that!evaluated!the!effect!of!IgG!supplementation!
to!dairy!calves!within!the!first!28!days!after!birth!on!diarrhoea,!weight!gain,!serum!IgG!levels!
and!morbidity.!
A!total!of!60!calves!from!a!single!Danish!dairy!herd!were!enrolled!in!the!study.!Calves!were!
randomly!assigned!to!one!of!three!treatment!groups.!The!treatment!groups!received!either!10!
grams! of! bovine! IgG,! 10! grams! of! porcine! IgG! or! no! supplementary! IgG! per! day.! Health!
parameters!were!evaluated!and!monitored!daily!and!calves!were!weighed!on!day!2,!7,!14,!21!
and!28!during!the!study!period!of!28!days.!Blood!samples!were!drawn!on!day!2!and!14!after!
birth!and!analysed!for!serum!IgG!by!competitive!ELISA.!
Supplementation!of!IgG!did!not!reduce!the!risk!of!having!diarrhoea!during!the!study!period.!
Average!daily!weight!gain!was!not!affected!by!supplementation!of!IgG.!No!difference!in!mean!
serum!IgG!was!observed!on!day!2!and!14!after!birth!and!no!difference!in!the!risk!of!having!
pneumonia!could!be!detected.!
In!addition!to!the!clinical!field!trial!a!literature!study!was!carried!out!to!establish!background!
knowledge!about!diarrhoea!and!its!aetiology!as!well!prevention!and!treatment!of!diarrhoea.!

Introduction!
Calves!are!born!without!a!functional!immune!system,!due!to!the!synepitheliochorial!placenta!
of!cattle!not!allowing!transfusion!of!immunoglobulin!from!the!dam’s!circulation!to!that!of!the!
calf!(Chucri!et!al.,!2010).!Therefore!the!calf!must!consume!an!adequate!amount!of!colostrum!
to!ensure!passive!transfer!(FPT)!of!immunoglobulin!(Quigley,!2004).!A!cutQoff!value!of!<10!
mg/ml!of!IgG!in!serum!or!plasma!has!been!used!by!several!authors!to!define!FPT!(Arthington!
et!al.,!2000;!Beam!et!al.,!2009a;!Murphy!et!al.,!2014).!The!importance!of!feeding!good!quality!
colostrum!is!common!knowledge!for!almost!everyone!who!is!working!with!calves.!!However!a!
large! number! of! calves! in! the! modern! dairy! industry! still! experience! failure! of! passive!
transfer.!Several!studies!have!shown!prevalence!of!FPT!ranging!from!19.2!per!cent!to!38!%!
(Beam!et!al.,!2009b;!Panousis!et!al.,!2013;!Vogels!et!al.,!2013).!!
Successful!transfer!of!immunoglobulin!enhances!the!calf’s!ability!to!avoid!infectious!disease!
(Stilwell!and!Carvalho,!2011).!One!of!the!major!infectious!diseases!is!calf!diarrhoea!(Cho!and!
Yoon,!2014).!Diarrhoea!causes!reduced!animal!welfare,!reduced!performance!of!animals!and!
increased!costs!for!producers.!Previous!advice!on!preventing!calf!diarrhoea!has!focused!on!
minimizing!the!infectious!dose!the!calf!is!exposed!to!and!optimizing!colostrum!quality!and!
handling!to!ensure!adequate!passive!immunization!(Lorenz,!2009).!Also!vaccination!of!dry!
cows!and!heifers,!to!enhance!the!quality!of!their!colostrum,!is!widely!used!in!dairy!industry!
(Fernandez! et! al.,! 1998).! Despite! a! great! effort! a! lot! of! calves! still! need! treatment! for!
diarrhoea.!The!treatment!is!most!often!based!on!fluid!replacement,!nonQsteroidal!treatment!
and!antimicrobial!treatment!(Bradford,!2008).!
The! use! of! antimicrobials! in! farm! animals! is! a! hot! topic! right! now! because! of! increasing!
problems!with!resistant!bacteria.!The!farm!industry!has!a!great!interest!in!reducing!the!use!of!
antibiotics! in! order! to! maintain! consumers’! faith! in! the! food! they! produce.! New! ways! of!
preventing!calf!diarrhoea!and!thereby!reduce!use!of!antibiotics,!improve!animal!welfare!and!
economy!is!consequently!more!than!welcome.!This!paper!focuses!on!causes!of!diarrhoea!and!
alternative!methods!that!could!improve!intestinal!and!general!health!of!dairy!calves.!!!

Diarrhoea!definition!and!aetiology!
Diarrhoea!is!a!symptom!of!disease!with!a!multifactorial!aetiology.!Several!factors!can!lead!to!
diarrhoea.!Infectious!factors!include!bacteria,!virus!and!protozoa.!E.)coli,!salmonella!species,!
rota!and!corona!virus!are!commonly!associated!pathogens!of!bacterial!and!viral!diarrhoea.!
Major!protozoal!pathogens!are!cryptosporidium!species!and!Eimeria)species)(Bradford,!2008;!
Cho!and!Yoon,!2014).!Based!on!the!clinical!signs!it!is!not!possible!to!establish!an!aetiological!
diagnosis!and!most!often!the!disease!will!be!a!result!of!more!than!one!of!these!pathogens.!
Diarrhoea!is!defined!as!loss!of!body!fluids!and!electrolytes!that!excess!the!intake!of!fluid!and!
electrolytes.!This!results!in!dehydration!that!can!subsequently!lead!to!electrolyte!imbalances.!
There!are!two!pathophysiologic!different!types!of!diarrhoea.!A!hyperQsecretory!diarrhoea!in!
which!secretions!from!the!gut!exceed!the!absorptive!capacity!of!the!intestine.!A!malabsorptive!
form!where!secretions!are!normal,!but!the!intestinal!absorptive!capacity!is!altered!(Bradford,!
2008;!Cho!and!Yoon,!2014).!

Rota:!and!coronavirus!
Rotavirus!(BRV)!belongs!to!the!family!Reovirideae.)They!are!spherical!virus!with!segmented!
double!stranded!RNA.!There!are!seven!serogroups!of!BRV!(A!to!G),!with!serogroup!A!being!the!
most!prevalent.!Group!A!is!major!pathogen!associated!with!calf!diarrhoea!(Ramig,!2004;!Cho!
and!Yoon,!2014).!!
Bovine!coronavirus!(BCV)!belongs!to!the!family)Coronavidiae.)They!are!crown!like!pleomorph!
virus!with!single!stranded!RNA.!BCV!causes!diarrhoea!in!neonates,!but!can!also!infect!adult!
cattle! and! cause! winter! dysentery.! BCV! can! also! be! involved! in! bovine! respiratory! disease!
(Quinn,!2001).!
BRV!and!BCV!are!found!endemically!in!bovines.!Adult!animals!can!carry!and!shed!the!virus!
without! clinical! symptoms.! Carrier! animal! are! believed! to! be! one! a! common! source! of!
infection!for!newQborn!calves!(Bradford,!2008).!The!faecal!oral!route!is!the!main!route!for!
transmission!of!BRV!and!BCV,!however!BCV!can!also!be!isolated!from!the!respiratory!tract!of!
calves! (Clark,! 1993;! Cho! and! Yoon,! 2014).! Both! BRV! and! BCV! replicate! in! the! villous!
enterocytes!of!the!small!intestine!and!cause!the!infected!cell!to!die.!The!dead!enterocytes!are!
then!replaced!by!cuboidal!or!squamous!epithelium,!which!do!not!have!the!same!absorptive!
capacity! as! the! villous! enterocytes.! Also,! these! immature! cells! do! not! have! the! ability! to!
secrete!digestive!enzymes,!which!can!lead!to!bacterial!overgrowth!due!to!undigested!lactose.!

Protozoa!
Cryptosporidium) parvum) is! a! protozoa! often! associated! with! diarrhoea! in! cattle.! Calves!
become!infected,!by!ingesting!a!sporulated!oocyst.!C.)parvum)infects!the!superficial!cell!lining!

atrophy!or!destruction.!In!the!host!C.)parvum!goes!through!several!life!cycles!and!replicates,!
before!new!oocysts!are!being!shed.!Infected!animals!start!shedding!oocyst!as!soon!as!two!post!

Management!factors!that!can!influence!occurrence!of!diarrhoea!
As!previously!mentioned!dystocia!can!influence!the!occurrence!of!diarrhoea.!This!indicates!
that!reducing!the!prevalence!of!dystocia!would!subsequently!lead!to!improved!ability!to!resist!
infectious! disease! (Lombard! et! al.,! 2007).! Risk! factors! associated! with! dystocia! in! cattle!
include! fetopelvic! disproportion,! fetal! malposition,! uterine! inertia,! and! cervical! or! vaginal!
stenosis!(Mee,!2008).!
Both!fetal!size!and!the!size!of!the!dam!affect!the!rate!of!foetopelvic!disproportion!(Mee,!2008).!
At!a!herd!level!attention!should!therefore!be!on!body!condition!score!(BCS)!of!the!dam!and!
enhancing!growth!in!heifers,!so!they!achieve!and!adequate!body!size!and!BCS!at!the!time!of!
calving!(Mee!et!al.,!2011).!The!selection!of!sires!also!affects!the!size!of!the!calf.!!Hansen!et!al.!
(2004)!showed!significant!effect!of!breed!on!the!size!of!the!calf.!!
Proper!nutrition!of!dry!cows!is!also!essential!to!reduce!the!risk!of!hypocalcaemia!and!alter!the!
BCS!of!the!dam.!Both!high!BCS!and!hypocalcaemia!can!cause!uterine!inertia,!where!the!cow!

Treatment!and!prevention!
There!is!currently!no!medical!treatment!for!diarrhoea!of!viral!and!protozoal!origin.!Bacterial!
diarrhoea! caused! by! salmonella) spp.! and! E.) coli! seems! to! have! some! susceptibility! to!
antimicrobial!treatment.!!Therefore!treatment!of!diarrhoea!is!generally!supportive.!The!main!
goal! of! the! treatment! is! to! prevent! or! reverse! dehydration,! which! can! lead! to! acidQbase!
disturbances,!such!as!metabolic!acidosis.!This!can!be!done!either!by!oral!fluids,!containing!
electrolytes!or!in!severe!cases!intravenous!infusion!of!fluids!(Bradford,!2008).!
Preventative! methods! are! similar! to! that! of! other! contagious! disease.! Reducing! pathogen!
exposure!is!an!effective!means!of!prevention.!In!a!farm!setting,!this!can!be!done!by!having!
cows!give!birth!in!individual!pens,!rather!than!group!pens.!The!calf!should!be!removed!from!
the!dam!as!soon!as!possible!after!birth!and!put!in!a!properly!cleaned!and!disinfected!pen,!with!
sufficient! bedding.! Calf! pens! or! hutches! should! be! cleaned! and! disinfected! in! between!
individual! animals.! Groups! of! calves! should! be! kept! stable! an! avoid! mixing! of! older! and!
younger!animals!(Lorenz,!2009).!
Feeding!utensils!and!personnel!represents!a!potential!risk!of!spreading!disease.!Care!should!
therefore!be!taken!to!keep!strict!hygiene!standards!for!the!utensils!used!and!personnel!should!
wear!clean!clothing!and!wash!hands!or!use!gloves!when!taking!care!of!animals!with!clinical!
symptoms! of! diarrhoea.! Sick! calves! should! also! be! isolated! from! other! animals! (Bradford,!
2008;!Lorenz,!2009).!
Passive!immunization!!
Bovine! colostrum! synthesis! occurs! during! the! late! pregnancy.! It! contains! numerous!
immunologically! active! components,! such! as! immunoglobulins,! plasmacytes,! macrophages!
and!cytokines,!along!with!nutrients!required!by!the!neonate.!Bovine!colostrum!contains!IgG,!
IgA!and!IgM,!with!IgG!as!the!main!immunoglobulin!(Larson!et!al.,!1980).!!

Absorption!
IgG!is!absorbed!by!enterocytes!in!the!small!intestine,!with!the!most!absorption!occurring!in!
the!distal!part!of!the!small!intestine.!IgG!enters!the!enterocytes!by!pinocytosis,!which!allows!
enterocytes!to!nonQselectively!absorb!proteins!and!other!macromolecules.!Once!in!the!cell!a!
vacuole!containing!the!absorbed!IgG!is!formed.!This!vacuole!is!then!exocytosed!to!the!lymph!
system!(Staley!and!Bush,!1985).!The!lymph!drains!into!the!blood!circulation,!and!systemic!IgG!
can! now! be! detected! in! serum! or! plasma.! The! absorptive! capacity! of! the! intestine! rapidly!
decreases! after! birth.! It! is! believed! that! this! decline! is! caused! by! natural! turnover! of!
enterocytes.!Also!the!secretion!of!digestive!enzymes!increases!after!birth,!and!IgG!undergoes!
some!enzymatic!digestion,!thereby!reducing!the!amount!of!IgG!that!can!readily!be!absorbed!
(Staley!and!Bush,!1985).!If!the!amount!of!good!quality!colostrum!is!not!sufficient!it!can!be!
considered! to! use! colostrum! substitutes! or! replacers.! Several! commercial! replacers! are!

Introduction!
!
!
Calf! diarrhoea! is! an! important! disease! in! preweaned! calves! in! modern! dairy! industry.!
Diarrhoea!can!cause!substantial!economic!losses!to!farmers!because!of!reduced!performance!
of! diarrheic! animals,! time! consuming! treatments! and! mortality.! Diarrheic! animals! become!
dehydrated,!experience!weight!loss!and!malnutrition,!which!subsequently!leads!to!reduced!
animal!welfare.!(Cho!and!Yoon,!2014).!
Calves! are! born! without! immunoglobulin! and! rely! on! consuming! an! adequate! amount! of!
colostrum!high!in!IgG!to!facilitate!passive!immunization.!(Quigley,!2004;!Chucri!et!al.,!2010).!
However,!IgG!in!the!intestinal!lumen!does!also!aid!in!protecting!the!calf!against!infection!with!

!The!use!of!antibiotics!for!treating!diarrhoea!has!been!subject!to!public!attention!in!the!recent!
years!because!of!concerns!about!antimicrobial!resistance.!Alternative!methods!for!preventing!
and!treating!diarrhoea!are!therefore!being!investigated.!Several!studies!in!different!species!
have! shown! that! the! addition! of! bovine! colostrum! or! porcine! plasma! to! feed! can! improve!
intestinal!health!(Van!Dijk!et!al.,!2002a;!Berge!et!al.,!2009;!Støy!et!al.,!2014).!It!is!therefore!
hypothesised!that!this!would!also!apply!to!diarrhoea!in!preweaned!dairy!calves.!
A!field!trial!including!60!calves!that!where!randomly!assigned!to!three!treatment!groups,!was!
carried!out!to!investigate!this.!Treatment!groups!received!10!gram!of!IgG!every!day;!either!as!
bovine!colostrum!powder!or!porcine!plasma!derived!IgG!in!addition!to!the!milk!feeding.!A!

Statistical!analysis!
All!observations!were!recorded!on!paper!and!transferred!to!a!spreadsheet!in!Microsoft!Office!
Excel®.!Descriptive!statistics!were!primarily!carried!out!using!Excel.!Statistical!analyses!were!

and!univariate!procedure.!Finally!oneQway!ANOVA!was!also!used.!

To!evaluate!the!effect!of!group!on!the!risk!of!having!diarrhoea!or!pneumonia!during!the!study!
period!of!28!days!for!each!calf!two!logistic!models!were!built.!The!data!from!the!diarrhoea!
score! was! converted! into! a! binary! variable! for! each! calf! for! the! entire! study! period.! Only!

risk.!!The!risk!of!disease!was!evaluated!by!calculating!odds!ratios,!with!the!control!group!as!

Discussion!
Diarrhoea!
!
Initially!diarrhoea!assessment!was!supposed!to!be!based!on!daily!faecal!scores!from!each!calf.!
However!it!was!not!possible!to!use!this!method,!because!it!was!difficult!to!find!faeces!from!
each!calf!and!it!would!be!too!time!consuming!to!perform!rectal!digital!exploration!of!each!calf!
every! day.! Moreover! it! was! considered! to! be! associated! with! discomfort! to! the! calves.!
Consequently!another!scale!for!assessing!diarrhoea!was!invented!during!the!first!days!of!the!
study! period.! To! test! the! hypothesis! the! date! from! this! scale! was! converted! into! a! binary!
outcome!for!the!whole!study!period.!A!pilot!study!or!an!additional!visit!of!the!herd!prior!to!the!
study!would!have!been!beneficial,!since!an!alternative!method!of!assessing!diarrhoea!could!
have!been!used!from!the!beginning!of!the!study.!
The!risk!of!having!diarrhoea!did!not!differ!in!between!groups.!This!is!contrary!to!what!other!
studies! have! reported.! ! Berge! et! al.! (2009)! evaluated! the! effect! of! feeding! colostrum!
supplementation,!for!dairy!calves!in!the!first!14!days!of!life,!and!found!that!it!reduced!the!
overall!risk!of!experiencing!diarrhoea.!However!the!doses!in!the!study!by!Berge!et!al.!(2009)!
was!higher!than!in!this!study.!Research!in!other!species!have!also!shown!beneficial!effects!to!
intestinal!health,!by!feeding!either!colostrum!or!plasma!products!(Van!Dijk!et!al.,!2002b;!Støy!
et!al.,!2014).!
A!possible!explanation!to!the!fact!that!IgG!supplementation!did!not!reduce!the!risk!of!getting!
diarrhoea!could!be!that!the!high!level!of!management!at!the!farm!reduces!the!exposure!to!
pathogens.!!The!farm!pasteurizes!the!milk;!wash!the!pails!after!each!milk!feeding!and!all!pens!
are!washed!in!between!different!calves.!All!of!these!measures!are!believed!to!have!reduced!
the! pathogen! exposure.! Furthermore! supportive! treatment! was! often! initiated! even! before!
calves! showed! clinical! symptoms! of! diarrhoea,! which! could! reduce! the! clinical! signs! of!
diarrhoea.!Hence!the!possible!beneficial!effect!of!IgG!supplementation!could!be!reduced,!due!
to!the!supportive!treatment!enabling!the!calf!to!resist!infection.!This!approach!to!treatment!of!
diarrhoea!applied!to!all!of!the!three!treatment!groups.!
Failure!of!passive!transfer!has!been!shown!to!increase!the!risk!of!disease!in!calves!(Stilwell!
and!Carvalho,!2011;!Cho!and!Yoon,!2014).!However!in!this!herd!serum!IgG!levels!less!than!10!

mg/ml!did!not!affect!the!risk!of!having!diarrhoea!or!other!disease.!This!further!supports!the!
theory!that!the!high!level!of!management!prevented!disease.!
During!the!study!no!diagnostic!investigations!were!performed!to!determine!what!pathogens!

There! were! also! some! limitations! to! the! statistical! method! used! for! evaluating! diarrhoea.!

Average!daily!weight!gain!
!
Weight!data!from!each!calf!was!obtained!at!the!same!time!of!the!day!for!all!calves!to!ensure!
that!weights!were!comparable!between!calves.!During!the!first!two!weeks!weighing!was!done!
with! a! modified! pig! scale.! However! the! scale! was! too! small! as! calves! grew! bigger! and!
potentiated!the!need!for!a!larger!scale.!The!larger!scale!was!from!the!same!manufacturer!and!
used!same!technology!as!the!first!scale.!Furthermore!the!calibration!of!the!two!scales!was!
carried!out!in!the!same!manner!and!it!was!not!considered!that!it!would!coincide!with!the!
result!that!two!different!scales!were!used.!!
There! was! no! statistically! significant! difference! in! average! daily! weight! gain! in! between!
groups,!which!is!contrary!to!a!previous!study!by!Berge!et!al.!(2009).!They!found!that!oral!
supplementation!of!IgG!during!the!first!14!days!of!life!was!associated!with!increased!average!
daily! weight! gain.! However! it! fits! with! the! results! regarding! diarrhoea,! as! no! significant!
difference!in!the!risk!of!having!diarrhoea!could!be!shown.!!

amount! of! milk! and! it! is! assumed! that! the! higher! birth! weights! of! the! crossbred! calves!
reduced!the!amount!of!energy!per!kilo!body!weight,!than!smaller!calves.!The!fact!that!being!a!
twin!had!such!large!impact!on!the!intercept!of!the!random!intercept!and!random!slope!model,!

Vogels!et!al.!(2013)!used!serum!total!protein!to!quantify!IgG.!!On!the!farm!where!the!study!

All!calves!receive!an!equal!amount!of!colostrum!in!a!timely!manner.!However!upon!further!

!It!was!a!surprise!that!so!many!calves!experienced!FPT,!even!though!all!calves!received!4!litres!

Morbidity!
!
The! number! of! cases! of! omphalitis,! arthritis! and! other! diseases! was! low,! so! no! statistical!
analysis!was!performed!to!test!of!these!diseases!were!influenced!by!oral!supplementation!of!
IgG.!!
Pneumonia!however!occurred!more!frequently!and!was!analysed!in!the!same!manner!as!for!
diarrhoea.!There!was!a!tendency!that!male!calves!and!calves!in!the!bovine!and!porcine!group!
had!reduced!risk!of!having!pneumonia.!The!male!calves!were!housed!in!another!location!than!
female!calves!for!approximately!the!first!14!days!of!their!lives.!No!older!calves!are!housed!at!
this!calfQrearing!site,!whereas!female!calves!are!housed!in!close!proximity!to!older!calves.!It!is!
therefore!hypothesised!that!female!calves!experience!greater!exposure!to!airway!pathogens!
than! do! male! calves! through! the! whole! study! period! than! female! calves.! The! bovine! and!
porcine! groups! also! had! more! male! calves! than! the! control! group,! which! could! possible!
explain!why!both!sex!and!treatment!group!tended!to!reduce!the!risk!of!pneumonia.!However!
when!male!calves!are!approximately!14!days!old!they!are!grouped!at!the!same!site!as!the!
female!calves,!so!the!difference!would!have!been!reduced!at!that!time.!!

Conclusion!
In!this!field!trial!the!oral!supplementation!of!IgG!did!not!affect!the!risk!of!having!diarrhoea!
during!the!first!28!days!of!life.!Furthermore!it!did!not!affect!the!growth!rate!of!the!calves.!
Serum!IgG!levels!were!not!statistically!different!between!the!three!treatment!groups.!
There! was! a! tendency! that! calves! that! received! porcine! IgG! had! reduced! risk! of! having!
pneumonia!during!the!study!period.!However!this!reduction!could!not!be!attributed!to!the!
oral! supplementation! of! IgG.! The! study! population! in! this! particular! field! trial! was!
heterogeneous!in!terms!of!sex,!breed!and!the!distribution!of!twins!in!the!treatment!groups.!
This!might!have!affected!the!result!and!further!investigation!with!a!more!homogenous!study!
population!is!warranted!in!order!to!reduce!the!possible!effects!of!this!heterogeneity.!
!
Perspective!
The! evaluation! of! the! effect! of! feeding! supplementary! IgG! on! diarrhoea,! weight! gain! and!
morbidity! in! dairy! calves! is! not! complete! by! means! of! this! study.! It! can! be! seen! as! an!
inspiration!to!further!investigation!that!involves!several!different!doses!of!IgG!and!possibly!
conducted!at!several!farms!instead!of!just!one!farm.!This!would!provide!more!evidence!about!
the!effect!of!IgG.!Furthermore!future!studies!could!include!diagnostics!of!diarrheic!pathogens!
and!the!qualitative!analyses!of!the!supplemenetary!IgG.!
