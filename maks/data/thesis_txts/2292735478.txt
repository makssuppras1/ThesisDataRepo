ABSTRACT'
With!the!entrance!of!China!in!the!WTO,!the!world!got!to!enter!and!compete!on!the!Chinese!
market,!but!at!the!same!time!Chinese!companies!from!all!industries!could!start!pursuing!
international! recognition.! This! assignment! studies! some! of! these! Chinese! companies,! and!
identifies!their!engagements!in!internationalization!and!global!product!development!(GPD).!
Moreover,!the!strategies!employed,!and!the!motivations!and!factors!influencing!the!choice!
of!strategies!are!identified,!as!well!as!possible!linkages!between!the!internationalization!and!
the!global!product!development!processes.!
To!acquire!the!information!and!knowledge!needed!to!write!the!thesis,!four!interviews,!in!
three! different! Chinese! manufacturing! companies,! were! conducted! with! appropriate!
Chinese!personnel.!From!the!procured!data,!an!analysis!using!the!theories!and!model!was!
conducted!in!each!company.!Followed!by!crossGcase!analysis!to!find!any!and!all!similarities!
and!differences.!
The! results! from! the! case! companies! show! that! Chinese! companies! have! become!
experienced!
in!
maneuvering!
on!
the!
international!
market!
and!
conducting!
internationalization,! but! that! the! discipline! of! performing! and! handling! global! product!
development!processes!is!still!a!new!area!where!learning!and!experience!can!be!gained.!
Chinese!companies!have!in!recent!years!been!more!focused!on!“going!out”.!The!Chinese!
foreign!interests,!along!with!a!Western!market!with!a!desire!for!companies!with!money!to!
invest,! have! given! to! chance! seeking! Chinese! firms,! a! new! market! to! expand! on.! The!
motivations! for! this! have! not! only! been! the! market! itself,! but! also! the! knowledge! (e.g.!
organization,! management,! communication…)! and! technology! (e.g.! new! machines,!
components,! products…)! and! resources! available! in! those! foreign! markets.! These!
competencies!have!been!used!to!further!improve!international!awareness,!or!to!enhance!
competitiveness!on!the!home!market.!
The!research!carried!out,!should!be!further!expanded!upon,!using!a!larger!sample!size!to!see!
if!the!tendencies!found!in!this!assignment!holds!true!on!a!larger!scale.!Looking!into!certain!
industries!and!companies!with!specific!offset!and/or!size!could!also!be!a!possibility.!
Overall,!the!results!show!similarities,!but!also!differences,!to!the!studies!conducted!by!other!
researchers,!which!means!that!these!results,!can!help!to!give!an!improved!understanding!of!
what!internationalization!and!global!product!development!is!and!what!it!means!for!Chinese!
manufacturing!companies.!

“…a$slumbering$giant$that$was$better$left$alone,$because$if$stirred$it$

His"reference"at"the"time"referred"to"the"enormity"of"the"country"and"its"many"people."Today"
