Abstract 
The!quality!of!the!Danish!property!tax!system!in!terms!of!taxable!value!and!land!taxation,!has!
been! questioned! and! debated! for! several! years.! The! main! issues! are! the! lack! of! accuracy,!
precision! and! public! comprehensibility.! In! 2013,! the! Ministry! of! Taxation! tried! to! deal! with!
these!issues!by!ordering!a!group!of!experts,!the!Engberg!Commission,!to!improve!the!existing!
property!tax!system.!However,!even!though!the!Engberg!Commission!managed!to!make!slight!
improvements!to!the!existing!property!tax!system,!their!solution!was!turned!down.!
!
The!aim!of!this!thesis!is!to!develop!the!Danish!property!tax!system!for!private!properties!and!
the!appertaining!valuation!algorithm,!through!data!analysis!of!building!parameters.!Focusing!on!
accuracy,! precision,! public! comprehensibility,! socioeconomic! consequences! and! the! effect! on!
the!existing!building!mass.!
!
Through!a!systematic!iterative!design!processes,!a!national!scale!data!analysis!has!been!carried!
out,! based! on! an! empirical! generalisation.! All! data! has! been! managed! and! analysed! using!
Microsoft!Excel!with!the!Frontline!Systems’!Premium!Solver!Pro!plugin.!
!
This!thesis!proposes!an!improved!property!tax!and!valuation!system!L!the!Property!Valuation!
Tool! (PVT),! in! order! to! improve! the! accuracy! of! the! taxable! value.! This! property! tax! system!
introduces! an! emission! tax,! as! a! replacement! for! the! land! taxation,! which! is! based! on! the!
theoretical!CO2!emission!of!each!individual!property.!
The! PVT! system! has! an! overall! adjusted! hitLrate! of! 60%! (based! on! national! distribution! of!
properties).!That!is!15%!better!than!the!current!valuation!system!from!SKAT.!!
The!test!results!of!the!PVT!system!is!divided!into!4!categories,!each!with!5!subcategories,!and!
then! compared! to! SKAT! using! a! total! of! 155! parameters.! The! result! is! this:! the! PVT! is! more!
accurate!than!SKAT!within!125!of!these!155!parameters.!
The!PVT!valuation!could!result!in!an!increased!property!tax!income!of!20%!(barring!a!repeal!of!
the! tax! freeze)! L! due! to! greater! accuracy! in! the! under! assessed! properties.! Only! 11%! of! the!
properties!evaluated!with!PVT!can!expect!a!wrongful!tax!increase!(relative!to!the!2014!SKAT!
assessment).!
The!emission!tax!is!designed!to!return!the!same!tax!income!to!each!individual!municipality!as!
the! land! taxation! (in! terms! of! GDP),! and! the! emission! tax! from! the! individual! properties! are!
locally!comparable!within!the!municipality!(contrary!to!the!land!taxation).!
!
It!is!indeed!possible!to!develop!the!current!property!taxation!system!to!improve!the!hitLrate.!
However,! due! to! market! values! being! based! on! supply! and! demand,! it! will! be! impossible! to!
design!a!system!with!an!accuracy!of!100%!and!a!precision!of!±20%.!However,!the!key!to!an!
accurate! system,! is! a! sufficient! amount! of! observations! within! a! uniform! and! well! defined!
region.!
!
Keywords 
Property! tax,! taxable! value,! land! taxation,! emission! tax,! iterative! process,! data! analysis,!
empirical!generalisation,!development!of!algorithms,!CO2!emission!

2.1.1 Taxable value 
The! taxable! value! replaced! the! rental! value! tax! in! 2000,! and! it! is! therefore! a! relatively! new!
element!in!the!property!tax!system.!It!is!paid!with!the!argument!that!the!property!represents!an!
investment!similar!to!bonds.!Each!year!the!owner!of!a!bond!will!(ideally)!receive!interests.!The!
owner!of!a!property!will!not!receive!interests!in!form!of!money!L!but!will!instead!earn!the!right!
to! occupy! the! property,! and! that! is! L! in! regards! to! tax! calculation! L! considered! interest.!
Meanwhile,!the!owner!of!the!property!might!be!able!to!sell!the!property!with!a!profit!L!just!like!
the!owner!of!a!bond.!
!
But!regardless!if!one!owns!a!bond!or!a!property,!one!must!pay!tax!based!on!the!interest.!The!
“interest”!of!a!property!is!calculated!as!the!taxable!value.!
!
The! taxable! value! is! paid! by! the! owner! AND! resident! of! a! given! property.! This! tax! is! only!
collected!if!both!criteria!are!met.!
The!taxable!value!is!1.0%!of!the!first!DKK!3,040,000!L!and!then!3.0%!of!the!rest.!
!
*
E.g.*the*taxable*value*for*a*property*with*a*DKK*4,000,000*property*assessment*would*be:!
*
3,040,000*×*0.01*+*960,000*×*0.03*=*DKK*59,200!
!
The! property! assessment! is! based! on! the! (theoretical)! value! of! the! property,! and! is! updated!
every!second!year!(odd!years)!L!however,!due!to!the!tax!freeze!(see!“5.1.2*The*tax*freeze”)!L!only!
about!5%!of!Danish!properties!will!be!taxed!based!on!the!latest!assessment.!Most!properties!
will! still! use! the! old! valuation! from! 2001/2002! (since! they! tend! to! be! lower! than! the! latest!
assessments).!
!
This!tax!is!a!part!of!the!monthly!tax!payment!in!Denmark!L!and!it!should!therefore!automatically!
be!deducted!from!the!pay!check.!
!
However,! there! is! an! unpleasant! paradox! with! the! taxable! value! L! because! the! owner! of! the!
property!will!be!“punished”!when!improving!the!property!L!since!it!will!result!in!an!increased!
property!value.!
This!paradox!could!eventually!result!in!a!reduced!quality!of!the!building!mass,!if!the!owners!are!
unwilling!to!accept!an!increase!in!property!tax.!
!
A!way!to!deal!with!this!is!the!land!taxation!L!described!below.!
!

2.1.2 Land taxation 
The!land!taxation!is!based!on!Henry!George's2!ideas!of!“a*single*tax*on*land*values”!L!and!it!was!
implemented!in!the!Danish!taxation!system!in!1920.!
The!land!taxation!is!designed!to!reduce!speculation!and!volatility!in!the!market.!If!the!cost!of!
land!goes!up,!then!the!land!taxation!must!go!up!as!well!L!and!an!equivalent!reverse!process!must!
happen!if!the!cost!of!land!goes!down.!
!
When!the!cost!of!land!goes!up,!it!will!increase!speculation!L!and!it!might,!in!long!term,!create!a!
“bubble!effect”!(where!the!asking!price!exceeds!the!actual!value).!But!due!to!the!land!taxation,!
the! effect! of! the! increased! value! should! be! reduced! L! because! the! land! taxation,! in! theory,! is!
based!on!the!supply!and!demand!of!the!land.!
In!the!same!way,!the!land!taxation!“protects”!the!landowners!when!the!cost!of!land!goes!down.!
!
The!land!taxation!will!not!“punish”!citizens!who!choose!to!improve!their!property!L!since!this!
taxation!only!affects!the!land.!

The!land!taxation!is!a!municipal!tax!L!contrary!to!the!taxable!value.!The!tax!rate!is!therefore!
individually!determined!by!each!of!the!98!municipalities!in!Denmark.!By!law,!the!land!taxation!
must!be!within!the!range!16.00!L!34.00‰.!Gentofte!is!the!only!municipality!with!the!lowest!land!
taxation!rate!L!while!7!municipalities!have!the!highest!rate!(among!them!is!Copenhagen).!On!
average,!the!land!taxation!rate!is!26.25‰.!

Due!to!the!nominal!tax!freeze!L!the!land!taxation!system!is!rendered!ineffectual.!It!results!in!an!
increasing!value!of!the!land!and!it!favours!speculation.!

In! other! words,! as! long! as! the! tax! freeze! is! in! effect,! the! land! taxation! has! lost! its! original!
purpose!and!it!might!as!well!be!abandoned.!

In!relation!to!the!Danish!property!taxation!system,!the!most!interesting!element!is!the!“Land”!

The! system! is! applicable! on! both! private! homes! and! businesses! L! but! in! any! regard,! the!
promoter! of! the! system! is! supply! and! demand.! If! a! lot! is! located! in! a! popular! and! wellL

6.2.3 Emission tax 
The!comprehensibility!of!the!emission!tax!was!briefly!discussed!in!“5.2.6*Comprehensibility*of*
the*emission*tax”!L!however,!this!part!will!focus!on!the!actual!communication.!
!
The! emission! tax! is! replacing! the! land! taxation!L! which! is,! unlike! the! taxable! value,! collected!
biannually.! The! land! taxation! bill! is! very! unspecific,! and! it! often! only! contains! the! land!
assessment!and!the!area!specific!land!tax!rate.!No!information!is!given!regarding!the!origin!of!
the!land!assessment,!and!this!tax!can!therefore!be!very!difficult!to!comprehend!for!the!citizen.!!
!
In!order!to!be!a!success,!the!emission!tax!must!be!easy!to!understand.!The!calculation!method!is!
designed!to!be!as!simple!to!use!L!and!understand!L!as!possible.!It!makes!use!of!only!3!input!
parameters!that!any!property!owner!ought!to!know:!
!
● Area,!Liveable!
● Energy!Label!
● Heat!source!
!
The! input! is! used! in! the! formula! from! “5.2.1* Applying* the* system”* to! calculate! the! total! CO2!
emission.!The!calculation!is!based!on!the!energy!label!L!but!if!the!Danish!Energy!Agency!are!
against! the! use! of! energy! labels! in! relation! with! property! tax,! then! the! emission! can! also! be!
calculated!based!on!factual!energy!consumption!by!using!the!following!formula:!
!
!Total!CO2!emission = Energy!Consumption⋅Emissionheat!source!
!
Regardless!of!which!of!the!2!methods!that!will!be!used,!it!is!highly!dependent!on!the!heat!source!

shown!in!Table!5.5.!A!shortcut!to!a!low!emission!tax,!is!therefore!to!convert!to!district!heating!
(if!possible),!since!it,!as!a!minimum,!will!more!or!less!halve!the!CO2!emission.!
Solid! fuel! from! wood! etc.! is,! as! mentioned! earlier,! by! some! considered! to! be! CO2! neutral! L!
however,!as!long!as!this!is!unconfirmed,!the!CO2!emission!of!solid!fuel!will!be!calculated!using!
the!average!emission!factor!(Emissionheat*source!=!0.275!kg/kWh).!
However,!straw!is!still!considered!to!be!CO2!neutral,!and!if!straw!is!used!as!the!primary!heat!

6.2.4 Current level of communication from SKAT 
When!looking!at!the!current!level!of!communication!from!SKAT!L!then!it!appears!to!be!a!bit!
esoteric.! It! can! be! difficult! for! the! general! public! to! understand! the! different! aspects! of! the!
annual!tax!return.!
The!calculations!in!the!tax!return!often!consist!of!one!or!more!variables!(nominal!value!L!input)!
and! then! some! constants! (tax! rates).! The! latter! is! either! a! national! or! municipal! tax,! and! it!
should!be!fairly!simple!to!understand.!However,!the!nominal!values!can!be!more!complicated!to!
trace!L!since!the!source!and!calculation!method!are!taken!for!granted.!This!creates!confusion,!
raises! questions! L! and! maybe! even! complaints! L! and! it! will! eventually! result! in! additional!
administrative!work!for!the!SKAT!processing!team.!
!
The!new!calculation!method!for!the!taxable!value!and!emission!tax!should!therefore!be!easy!to!
understand! L! but! at! the! same! time,! blend! in! seamlessly! with! the! rest! of! the! tax! return.! It! is!
important!that!this!particular!part!is!not!accentuated!and!over!explained.!
However,!in!order!to!make!it!easier!for!the!citizen!to!research!the!terms!in!the!tax!return!form!L!
the! information! has! been! phrased! to! consist! of! the! words! “normalfordelte* kurve”! [normal!
distributed!curve]!and!“edderkoppediagram”![Spider!Diagram].!
!
Finally!it!is!important!that!the!SKAT!processing!team,!can!make!use!of!some!wellLdefined!tools,!
when!handling!property!tax!related!complains!L!and!that!there!is!a!clear!objective!method!to!
determine!whether!a!complaint!is!justified!or!not.!
The!taxable!value!is!the!mean!value!of!the!most!probable!sales!price!range,!based!on!a!given!
accuracy.!Only!if!the!owner!of!a!property!can!document!(e.g.!using!a!real!estate!agent!etc.),!that!
the!expected!sales!price!of!the!property!is!below!the!lower!boundary!range,!then!it!is!possible!to!
file!a!complaint.!
The!emission!tax!is!based!on!the!theoretical!CO2!emission!of!a!property,!and!it!should!therefore!
be!indisputable.!

6.2.5 The actual property value 
It! can! be! very! difficult! to! determine! the! actual! value! of! a! property.! But! ultimately! it! can! be!
divided!into!3!groups:!
!
● Asking!price!
● Sales!price!
● Algorithmic!assessment!(e.g.!SKAT/PVT)!
!
Asking!and!sales!prices!are!very!subjective.!The!asking!price!is!often!a!result!of!an!agreement!
between!a!real!estate!agent!and!the!owner!of!the!property!L!and!it!is!highly!subjective.!The!sales!
price!is!based!on!what!a!potential!buyer!is!willing!to!pay!L!often!(unconsciously)!determined!by!
supply!and!demand.!But!it!can!also!be!highly!influenced!by!an!uninformed!(overpaying)!buyer.!!
The! algorithmic! assessments! can! be! both! subjective! and/or! objective.! The! PVT! algorithm! is!
considered!to!be!totally!objective,!since!the!assessments!are!based!solely!on!actual!sales!prices!L!
e.g.! supply! and! demand.! One! could! therefore! argue! that! the! PVT! assessment! a! property! is! a!
more!accurate!expression!of!the!actual!value,!rather!than!the!sales!price!L!since!PVT!is!based!on!
hundreds!of!observations,!creating!a!more!uniform!valuation!relative!to!the!actual!sales!price.!

6.3 
Effect on existing building mass 
Predicting!the!effect!on!the!existing!building!mass!is!of!course!highly!speculative,!and!it!is!all!
based!on!the!assumption!that!the!system!will!be!embraced!by!the!general!public!L!and!work!as!
intended.!
!
There!is!definitely!room!for!profitable!energy!improvements!in!private!properties!in!Denmark!
(see!Figure!2.2).!One!of!the!main!benefits!from!the!emission!tax!is!that!it!can!trigger!the!public!
willingness!to!carry!out!profitable!energy!improvements.!The!emission!tax!is!not!in!any!way!
financing! the! improvements! L! however,! it! is! a! tool! that! might! encourage! people! to! invest! in!
energy!improvements.!If!an!owner!of!a!high!energy!consuming!property!chooses!to!do!without!
energy!improvements!L!then!it!is!likely!that!the!annual!tax!bill!will!increase,!relative!to!the!2014!
SKAT!land!taxation.!
!
It!is!possible!to!avoid!the!emission!tax,!by!installing!a!straw!boiler![halmfyr].!About!40%!of!the!
annual! Danish! straw! production! (2.4! million! tonnes)! is! left! unused! [Stenkjær! N.,! 2009]! L!
installing! straw! boilers! in! private! homes! would! therefore! be! both! a! CO2! reducing! and!
sustainable!solution.!
However,!a!straw!boiler!is!rather!maintenance!heavy!L!and!can!be!very!impractical.!Therefore!it!
is!considered!to!be!unlikely!that!a!huge!part!of!the!Danish!properties!will!switch!to!straw!boilers!
in!order!to!avoid!the!emission!tax.!However,!it!might!be!an!interesting!alternative!for!larger!
apartment!complexes!L!where!a!professional!caretaker!could!manage!the!boiler.!
!
In!time,!the!emission!tax!will!improve!on!the!overall!quality!of!the!Danish!building!mass,!it!will!
improve!the!indoor!climate,!and!reduce!the!CO2!emission.!
When!the!overall!building!mass!has!reached!an!acceptable!level!(e.g.!90%!of!the!properties!in!
energy!level!D!or!better),!the!emission!tax!can!be!phased!out,!and!replaced!by!an!undivided!
property!tax!system!L!i.e.!the!valuation!tax.!The!municipalities!would!then!receive!a!block!grant!
based!on!their!needs.!

A! total! of! 4! data! sets! were! selected! for! the! data! analysis,! based! on! their! representativeness!
relative! to! the! Danish! building! mass.! Each! data! set! represented! 200! randomly! selected!
properties,! sold! in! 2014.! Based! on! data! analysis,! using! empirical! generalisation,! a! property!
valuation!algorithm!was!designed,!through!a!series!of!iterations.!The!focus!of!each!iteration!was!
to!improve!the!accuracy!of!the!valuation!L!based!on!a!±20%!precision.!
The!final!algorithm!(the!Property!Valuation!Tool!L!PVT)!was!tested!and!compared!to!SKAT!using!
a!total!of!155!parameters.!It!turned!out!that!the!PVT!algorithm!was!more!accurate!than!SKAT!
within!125!of!these!155!parameters.!
The!PVT!valuation!could!result!in!an!increased!property!tax!income!of!20%!(barring!a!repeal!of!
the!tax!freeze)!L!mainly!due!to!greater!accuracy!in!the!under!assessed!properties.!Only!11%!of!
the! properties! evaluated! with! PVT! can! expect! a! wrongful! tax! increase! (relative! to! the! 2014!
SKAT!assessment).!
The!designed!algorithm!has!an!overall!adjusted!hitLrate!of!60%!(based!on!national!distribution!
of!properties),!which!is!15%!better!than!the!current!valuation!system!from!SKAT.!
!
The! land! taxation! appears! outdated,! due! to! lack! of! consistency! and! comparability! in! the!
valuations!L!an!emission!tax!was!therefore!introduced,!as!a!replacement!for!the!land!taxation.!
The! emission! tax! is! based! on! the! theoretical! CO2! emission! of! each! individual! property.! The!
emission!tax!is!designed!to!maintain!the!national!and!municipal!property!tax!income!L!and!is,!
unlike!the!land!taxation,!comparable!between!properties!within!the!municipality.!
The! emission! tax! will! benefit! property! owners! who! have! low! emitting! properties! and/or!
owners! with! a! relatively! high! 2014! SKAT! land! taxation.! Meanwhile,! the! emission! tax! should!
encourage! all! property! owners! to! make! profitable! energy! improvements,! since! such!
improvements!will!reduce!the!CO2!emission,!improve!the!indoor!climate,!raise!the!value!of!the!
property!and!ultimately!lower!the!emission!tax.!
!
In!the!annual!tax!return!form,!the!PVT!method!is!presented!in!a!clear!way!L!with!focus!on!how!
different!building!parameters!influence!the!final!valuation,!along!with!the!expected!sales!price!
range.! This! is! done! in! order! to! improve! the! public! comprehensibility! of! the! system! and! to!
reduce!the!number!of!complaints.!The!processing!team!at!SKAT!will!have!very!clear!and!specific!
guidelines!to!manage!property!valuation!related!complaints.!
Meanwhile,!the!emission!tax!will!be!presented!along!with!a!simple!formula,!explaining!how!the!
emission!tax!is!calculated!L!in!contrast!to!the!very!esoteric!land!taxation.!
!
It!is!indeed!possible!to!develop!the!current!property!taxation!system!to!improve!the!hitLrate.!
However,!due!to!market!values!being!based!on!supply!and!demand,!it!appears!impossible!to!
design!a!system!with!an!accuracy!of!100%!and!a!precision!of!±20%.!However,!the!key!to!an!
accurate! system,! is! a! sufficient! amount! of! observations! within! a! uniform! and! well! defined!
region.!
