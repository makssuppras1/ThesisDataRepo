Abstract 
!
Technology*has*the*potential*to*improve*life*quality,*but*the*challenges*present*when*introducing*
new*technology*to*developing*countries*is*often*so*immense*that*value*is*not*redeemed*because*
the*support*structures*surrounding*it*is*lacking.*1.2*billion*people*of*the*world*still*live*without*
access*to*electricity,*with*Sub?Saharan*Africa*hit*the*hardest.*Solar*photovoltaic*is*gaining*
increased*relevance*in*developing*countries,*and*in*East*Africa*pico?PV*providers*are*reaching*
previously*unreachable*user*segments*with*their*small?scale*solar*solutions.*The*solutions*use*a*
combination*of*technologies*like*Pay?As?You?Go*and*service*elements*to*support*the*PV*
technology,*hereby*creating*trust,*reliability*and*fostering*a*relationship*between*provider*and*user*
that*increases*the*value*creation.*The*system*with*which*the*providers*are*offering*their*solutions*
has*similarities*to*the*framework*of*Product?Service*Systems.*The*project*investigates*the*pico?PV*
case*of*East*Africa*by*examining*how*the*case*fits*within*the*PSS*framework*suggested*by*
Tukker’s*and*his*eight*different*PSS*types,*to*grant*insights*that*can*assist*in*the*understanding*of*
New*Technology*Introduction.*The*project*has*been*following*the*design*research*methodology*as*
a*type*2*project,*and*the*case*study*has*been*supported*by*expert*interviews*and*a*field*trip*in*the*
southern*part*of*Malawi.*The*work*shows*that*the*majority*of*the*cases*are*within*the*product?
related*service*type*or*the*lease*type,*where*users*either*have*ownership*of*the*solution*or*
constant*access.*Furthermore*it*shows*that*(i)*trust,*(ii)*breaking*the*cost*barrier*and*(iii)*service*
supporting*the*life*cycle*performance*of*the*technology,*are*important*aspects*for*the*user*when*
introduced*to*the*technology.*The*classification*also*showed*that*new*market*opportunities*could*
arise*within*the*sector,*illustrating*that*the*PSS*framework*can*be*used*as*a*tool*for*understanding*
by*not*only*highlighting*elements*of*relevance,*but*also*illustrating*examples*of*what*works*and*
how*it*works.**
*
Keywords:*Malawi,*East*Africa,*New*Technology*Introduction,*Product?Service*Systems*(PSS),*
photovoltaic,*pico*solar,*pico?PV,*design*research*methodology.**

1 Introduction 
When*designing,*producing*or*implementing*a*new*product*it*is*not*enough*that*it*works,*it*should*
also*function*as*intended.*To*accomplish*this,*the*context,*surroundings*and*the*dynamics*between*
user*and*provider*have*to*be*considered,*making*technology*very*dependent*on*social?technical*
dynamics.*It*is*not*a*given*that*the*value*a*technology*provides*to*one*area,*is*the*same*when*
transferred*to*another*area,*region*or*society.*Introducing*new*technology*to*developing*countries,*
this*becomes*even*more*evident,*which*numerous*examples*also*highlight.*It*is*not*uncommon*that*
technological*solutions*working*in*developed*countries*such*as*water*pumps,*buildings*or*electronic*
equipment*is*left*useless*in*developing*countries*due*to*reasons*connected*to*the*lesser*resources*
present.*Technological*progress*has*been*a*part*of*how*populations*improve*life*quality*and*build*
up*societies*for*ages.*Barriers*have*always*existed,*but*with*the*worlds*increasing*use*of*technology,*
understanding*the*barriers*is*more*relevant*than*ever.*The*introduction*of*technology*in*developing*
countries* is* experiencing* heavy* barriers* in* the* form* of* education,* institutions,* resources* and*
knowledge*sharing*(Banks,*Gondwe,*&*UNDP*Malawi,*2005).*Addressing*these*barriers*is*high*on*
the*global*agenda*when*dealing*with*global*sustainability*and*development.*
To* “ensure* access* to* affordable,* reliable,* sustainable* and* modern* energy* for* all”* is* one* of* the*
taglines* in* the* United* Nations’* mission* on* sustainable* development* and* an* area* with* great*
challenges*(UN,*2016).**Access*to*electricity*is*still*limited*in*many*parts*of*the*world*with*Sub?
Saharan*Africa*(SSA)*facing*the*most*severe*case.*According*to*Africa*Energy*Outlook*report*by*the*
International*Energy*Agency*(IEA,*2014)*it*is*the*only*part*of*the*world*where*the*number*of*people*
without*electricity*is*increasing,*amounting*to*more*than*600*million*people,*with*the*rural*population*
suffering*the*most.*Though*SSA*is*experiencing*heavy*challenges*when*it*comes*to*electricity,*a*
very*interesting*electricity*movement*is*taking*place.**
Solar*photovoltaic*(PV)*energy*systems*are*increasing*throughout*Africa,*and*especially*East*Africa*
is*seeing*a*large*number*of*solar*PV*in*the*form*of*so*called*pico?solar*or*pico?PV*systems.*Pico?PV*
is*defined*by*the*World*Banks*Lighting*Africa*initiative*as*small?scale*solar*portable*lights*(SPL)*or*
solar* house* systems* (SHS)* of* less* than* 15Wp.* Pico?PV* is* spreading* with* a* rapid* pace* and*
succeeding*in*adding*value*and*lifting*the*life*quality*of*its*users*to*the*extent*where*some*argue*that*
it*has*the*potential*to*“leapfrog”*and*bypass*fossil*fuel*based*energy*(Marquardt,*Steinbacher,*&*
Schreurs,*2016).*This*could*be*thought*of*as*analogous*to*how*the*mobile*phone*in*many*places*
bypassed*the*land?line.*The*expansion*of*pico?PV*is*happening*due*to*a*combination*of*external*
factors,*technology*development*and*business*innovation.*It*is*a*case*relevant*to*the*introduction*of*
new*technology,*and*is*therefore*also*the*focal*point*of*this*project*thesis.*

The* framework* surrounding* Product?
Service*Systems*(PSS)*will*be*used*to*
examine* the* pico?PV* case* of* East*
Africa* in* order* to* highlight* tendencies*
present*and*aiding*in*the*understanding*
of*technology*introduction*in*developing*
countries* in* the* work* to* overcome*
barriers.* PSS* is* a* way* of* designing*
business* solutions* based* on* holistic*
structures,* focusing* on* supporting* the*
life* cycle* performance* of* the* product.*
PSS*is*defined*by*leading*scholars*as*a*
solution*consisting*of*tangible*products*
and* intangible* services* that* combined*
support* each* other* in* a* way* that* fulfills*
specific*
customer*
demands*

5.2 Moving technology from state to state 
For*long,*the*idea*of*moving*technology*and*knowledge*between*countries*and*governments*was*
not*practiced*because*technology*in*many*cases*equaled*power.*It*was*not*until*the*end*of*World*
War*II*that*the*notion*of*technology*transfer*was*articulated,*making*it*a*relatively*new*process*taking*
place*since*the*middle*of*the*20th*century*(Siddiqi,*1990).*By*then*it*was*acknowledged*that*sharing*
could* bring* improvements,* and* that* modern* knowledge* was* a* tool* for* improving* life* quality* in*
general.**
Technology*is*in*classic*terms*perceived*as*a*commodity.*Something*that*can*be*reproduced*without*
cost*and*transmitted*from*one*agent*to*another,*like*making*a*copy*of*a*design*drawing*or*obtaining*
an* artifact* (Kranzberg,* 1986).* Today,* we* have* become* aware* that* it* is* not* that* simple,* since*
technology*is*perceived*as*knowledge,*brought*about*by*innovation*and*learning*from*others*(Mets,*
Davidson,*Martens,*van*Rooijen,*&*McGrory,*2000).**
It*is*important*to*distinguish*between*technology*and*science,*since*it*is*frequently*misinterpreted.*
Technology*often*leads*to*science*that*in*return*leads*to*new*technology.*Though*they*may*benefit*
from*each*other*they*are*different*on*some*important*aspects.*Science*is*the*raw*knowledge*and*is*
often*freely*obtainable*by*stakeholders,*whereas*technology*is*based*on*knowledge,*processes*and*
practices*and*often*private*(IPCC,*2000a*Mets*et*al.,*2000).*These*processes*and*practices*that*
simulate*the*way*knowledge*moves*between*individuals*and*organization*can*be*complex*at*times,*
which*is*in*fact*one*of*the*first*issues*of*effective*technology*transfer*(Dodgson*&*Bessant,*1996).*
This*underlines*the*importance*and*relevance*of*researching*within*the*process*of*transferring*and*
introducing*technology.*

5.3 New Technology Introduction (NTI) 
The*term*‘technology’*has*been*criticized*for*having*an*implicit*view*of*technology*as*an*object*and*
that* its* transfer* is* a* one?time* transaction* maintaining* the* dependency* of* the* recipient.* This* has*

technology*cooperation*are*two*terms*widely*used,*which*highlights*that*some*level*of*adaptation*is*

of*their*contribution*to*learning.**
All*stakeholders*understand*technology*transfer*differently,*which*is*an*important*recognition*and*
the* success* of* transfer* is* often* determined* by* policy* and* economic* adaptation* at* a* more*

emphasizes*that*a*new*context*and*dynamics*have*to*be*regarded,*focusing*on*the*value*related*to*

5.5 Dynamics of developing countries 
When*addressing*developing*countries,*it*is*important*to*understand*their*history*and*the*dynamics*

Most*developing*countries*are*old*colonies,*and*have*experienced*long*periods*of*occupation*and*
in*many*cases*suppression.*This*often*leads*to*less*developed*democratic*processes*and*high*levels*

7.3 Definitions 
PSS*literature*introduces*a*range*of*varying*PSS*definitions,*that*all*focus*on*the*aforementioned*
notion*of*working*towards*a*solution*rather*than*just*a*product.*Table*1)shows*10*definitions*of*
systems*from*1998*to*2015*with*that*exact*emphasis,*but*slightly*different*abbreviations*highlighted*
in*their*definitions*and*framework*names.*Seven*of*the*identified*definitions,*define*what*they*call*
Product?Service* Systems* (all* spelled* that* way).* The* remaining* three* are* named* Eco?efficient*
services,* Sustainable* Product?Service* Systems* and* Technical* Product?Service* Systems.* The*
variation* in* names* can* be* the* result* of* extended* emphasis* on* more* detailed* elements* or* other*
elements*relevant*for*the*author.*In*regards*to*Meijkamp*(1998)*and*Roy*(2000)*the*name*variations*
can*be*caused*by*the*fact*that*their*origin*is*from*around*the*same*time*that*the*term*PSS*was*
initially*coined,*and*that*the*environmental*aspects*had*not*been*thoroughly*investigated,*thus*the*
authors*probably*wanted*to*emphasize*more*specific*aspects.*However,*it*is*clear*from*the*way*they*
describe*their*definition*that*the*variations*resembles*the*other*PSS*definitions.*In*fact*Roy*(2000)*
suggests*one*of*only*two*definitions*that*include*all*three*of*the*identified*common*traits*that*cover*
the*10*definitions.*Three*elements*pop*up*in*several*of*the*definitions*literature*mentions*that*PSS*
is:*
1.* A*synergetic*system*between*tangible*products*and*intangible*services**
2.* Having*the*needs*of*the*consumer*in*focus*
3.* Prompting*a*shift*of*ownership*from*consumer*to*provider.**
*
As*seen*in*the*figure*every*definition*treats*the*mix*of*tangibles*and*intangibles,*giving*the*impression*

authors,*obtained*through*a*utilization*of*the*combined*support*that*both*products*and*services*can*
give*each*other.*Furthermore,*an*increased*focus*is*on*the*consumer*and*general*functionality,*
rather*than*on*a*product*designed*from*a*theoretical*standpoint.*This*seems*to*be*a*united*approach*
towards*creating*value?adding*solutions.*
*
Finally,* some* of* the* definitions* focus* on* who* obtains* ownership,* as* an* observed* effect* of* the*

highlights* an* essential* attention* to* the* ownership* situation.* In* summary,* the* similarities* in* the*

7.3.1 Environmental Sustainability 
The*general*perception*of*PSS*is*that*it*contributes*to*the*climate*change*debate*in*a*positive*way,*
since*it*addresses*emissions*through*its*benefits.*This*opinion*is*primarily*rooted*in*the*proposition*
that*the*main*benefit*of*PSS*is,*that*a*more*coherent*and*symbiotic*solution,*which*seeks*to*benefit*
the*social,*economic*and*sustainable*pillars,*is*created*(Manzini*&*Vezzoli,*2003).*Alongside*the*fact*
that*a*shift*in*ownership*gives*the*providers*means*to*be*in*better*control*of*the*resource*usage*as*
well* as* potential* shared* use* scenarios* of* the* products* through* a* system.* It* gives* them* an*
economically* rooted* incentive* to* optimize* material* and* energy* consumption* (Emili,* Ceschin,* &*
Harrison,*2016).**This*approach*is*easy*to*follow,*but*it*has*been*disputed*in*detail*through*literature.*
A*list*of*publications*advocate*that*PSS*is*offering*overall*environmental*improvements*(Roy,*2000a*
Yang,* Moore,* Pu,* &* Wong,* 2009)* while* others* are* less* inclined* to* state* it* up* front* without*
considerations*(Erkoyuncu*et*al.,*2011a*Wang*et*al.,*2011).*
*
Both*Erkoyuncu*et*al.*(2011)*and*before*that,*Tukker*(2004),*claim*that*environmental*benefit*relies*
on*the*details*of*the*PSS.*Some*PSS*systems*might*have*immediate*sustainable*benefits,*while*
others* may* increase* consumption* because* it* ends* up* being* another* “product”* or* change*
consumption*patterns,*a*so*called*rebound*effect*(Meijkamp,*1998).*This*leads*Tukker*to*conclude*
that* “PSS! does! not! equal! lower! environmental! impact! unless! sustainability! considerations! are!
factored! in! thoroughly! in! PSS! design”* (Tukker* &* Tischner,* 2006),* even* though* the* same* team*
previously*had*concluded*that*PSS*is*an*excellent*vehicle*to*foster*sustainability*(Tukker,*2003).*
This*indicates*the*difficulties*concerning*the*term*environmental*sustainability*in*relation*to*PSS*and*
in*general.*Due*to*the*complexities*surrounding*environmental*sustainability,*it*is*not*a*part*of*this*
project,*but*it*is*an*important*aspect*of*understanding*the*culture*and*potential*value*of*PSS*in*detail.*
Instead*of*determining*whether*acts*are*sustainable*or*not,*this*project*will*describe*the*value*by*
laying* out* examples* of* elements* like* environmental* awareness* and* consumer* needs,* using*
sustainability*as*a*broader*moniker.*
*

10 Conclusion 
Pico?PV*is*expanding*in*East*Africa*these*years,*through*several*providers*that*are*offering*the*
products*via*systems,*bearing*immediate*similarities*to*PSS.*The*case*of*pico?PV*thus*acted*to*
examine*the*application*of*PSS*in*the*work*with*New*Technology*Introduction*(NTI)*as*a*way*of*
better*understanding*it*in*the*context*of*developing*countries.*
An*investigation*of*the*introduction*of*new*technology*in*developing*countries*has*been*performed,*
with*the*Product?Service*Systems*(PSS)*approach*as*a*framework*for*increased*understanding.*NTI*
has* been* used* to* encompass* the* process* surrounding* the* arrival* of* technology* in* this* project,*

11 Limitations, Perspective and Further work 
One*element*that*is*mentioned*briefly*in*the*project*is*the*pico?PV*providers’*reliance*on*capital*and*
