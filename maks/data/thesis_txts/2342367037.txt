Abstract!
Waste! from! electrical! and! electronic! equipment! (WEEE)! is! one! of! the! fastest! growing! and!
complex!waste!streams!globally.!Within!WEEE,!endRofRlife!mobile!phones!are!highly!important!
because! of! several! reasons:! 1)! increasing! waste! generation! rates! and! short! lifespan;! 2)! low!
collection!rates;!and!3)!low!recycling!rates!of!its!valuable!materials!such!as!gold,!silver!or!rear!
earth! metals.! This! study! aimed! at! identifying! challenges! of! closing! material! flow! loop! and!
environmental!hotRspots!of!the!last!stage!of!mobile!phones’!life!cycle.!!
In!order!to!answer!these!questions,!life!cycle!assessment!(LCA)!was!performed!and!two!mobile!
phones!waste!treatment!scenarios!were!assessed!and!compared.!The!main!difference!of!the!
scenarios!was!that!scenario!1!was!modelled!including!waste!preRtreatment!process,!meanwhile,!
in!scenario!2!mobile!phones!were!sent!to!the!copper!smelter!directly.!The!method!used!to!
conduct!the!LCA!was!based!on!methodology!in!the!ISO!standards!14040!and!14044.!The!waste!
management! system! was! modelled! using! waste! LCA! program! –! EASETECH.! “ILDC!
recommended”!was!the!life!cycle!impact!assessment!method!used!to!evaluate!environmental!
impacts!and!savings!of!the!system.!!!
The! LCIA! results! revealed! that! recovering! the! metals! from! mobile! phones! results! in! the!
significant!environmental!savings!when!the!environmental!loads!are!credited!from!avoided!
production!of!the!same!amount!of!metals!by!mining!and!refining!the!ore.!Scenario!2!showed!
better!performance!than!scenario!1,!mainly!due!to!higher!amounts!of!metals!recovered.!When!
taking!into!the!account!only!environmental!loads!(without!substituting),!waste!preRtreatment!
and!transport!were!the!most!contributing!stages!in!scenario!1.!Meanwhile!in!scenario!2!it!was!
smelting! and! the! transport.! Overall! loads! to! the! impact! categories! from! both! scenarios!
contributed! evenly,! meaning! that! both! scenarios! performed! worse! in! the! same! amount! of!
impact!categories.!!
It!was!found!that!most!of!the!valuable!metals!are!lost!in!the!preRtreatment!step,!where!the!
recovery!rates!are!ranging!from!24%!for!palladium!to!95%!for!iron.!Moreover,!many!metals!
contained!in!the!mobiles!phones!nowadays!are!not!possible!to!recover!at!all.!!

3.!Theoretical!background!of!circular!economy!and!
accompanying!concepts!
!
Circular!economy!is!an!industrial!system,!where!the!main!goal!is!to!move!from!current!linear!
economic!model!to!circular.!Which!means!that!the!aim!is!to!keep!products!and!materials!at!
the!highest!utility!and!value!at!all!times!in!closed!material!loops.!Two!material!flow!cycles!are!
distinguished:!biological%cycle!and!technical%cycle.!Biological!cycle!is!designed!for!the!biological!
nutrients! to! reenter! biosphere! properly,! meanwhile! technological! cycle! is! designed! for!
technical!nutrients!to!circulate!in!the!system!without!entering!the!biosphere![31].!!!
According!to!Ellen!MacArthur!foundation,!the!origins!of!circular!economy!concept!cannot!be!
traced!back!to!a!specific!date!or!author.!The!concept!was!rather!formed!by!several!more!specific!
schools!of!thought!and!began!to!evolve!around!1970![31].!The!aspects!and!ideas!collected!in!
circular!economy!context!can!be!found!in!Michael!Braungart’s!and!Bill!McDonough’s!“Cradle!
to!Cradle”!concept,!Gunter!Pauli’s!“Blue!Economy”!movement,!Janine!Benyus’s!“Biomimicry”!
principles,!etc.!The!following!chapter!will!summarize!the!aboveRmentioned!concepts.!
3.1.'Circular'economy'
As!there!is!no!clear!recognition!what!circular!economy!involves!and!how!the!concept!can!be!
fulfilled,!the!circular!economy!model!defined!by!probably!the!most!known!and!widespread!
Ellen!MacArthur!Foundation!will!be!considered!in!this!study.!!
As! it! was! mentioned! before,! two! material! cycles! were! distinguished:! biological! cycle! and!
technical!cycle.!The!bioRcycle!materials!can!be!consumed;!meanwhile!technoRcycle!materials!
are! only! being! used.! Which! means! that! in! bioRcycle! the! resources! are! being! regenerated!
whereas!they!are!recovered!in!the!technical!one.!The!circular!economy!rests!on!three!principles!
found!by!Ellen!MacArthur!Foundation![32]:!
1.!
Preserve&and&enhance&natural&capital%
Circular!system!chooses!the!technologies!and!processes,!which!are!using!renewable!or!better!
performing!resources!and!encourages!the!flow!of!nutrients!to!circulate!in!the!system.!Thus!
creating!the!conditions!for!regeneration.!!
2.! Optimize&resource&yields&
Design!products!suitable!for!remanufacturing,!refurbishing!and!easy!recyclability!in!order!to!
keep!materials!circulating!in!the!economy!as!well!as!extending!the!lifespan!and!possibilities!to!
==PAGE:18==
reuse.!In!biological!cycle!products!are!designed!to!enter!the!biosphere!to!decompose!and!add!
a!new!resource!value!for!the!new!cycle.!!
3.! Foster&system&effectiveness&
System! should! be! designed! by! avoiding! negative! impacts! to! other! systems! such! as! food,!
education,! or! health! and! mitigating! environmental! externalities! such! as! pollution! of! toxic!
substances,!noise,!land!use,!water!and!air!use.!!

6.2.6.'Quality'and'representativeness'of'data'
According! to! ILCD! handbook,! representativeness! of! Life! Cycle! Inventory! data! is! a! key!
component!of!the!“data!quality”!concept![55].!The!recommended!structure!of!the!data!quality!
rests! on! three! main! criteria! attributes! –! temporal,! technological! and! geographical!
representatives.!!
Temporal)representativeness)
One!of!the!main!criterions!of!choosing!data!in!this!study!was!its!novelty.!However,!sometimes,!
older!data!was!prioritized!because!it!was!better!quality.!The!most!upRtoRdate!(from!2007)!found!
composition!of!the!mobile!phones!was!used.!Taking!into!the!account!that!the!average!life!span!
of!the!mobile!phone,!as!it!was!mentioned!before,!is!2R4!years!and!endRofRlife!phones!are!often!
kept!at!home,!it!can!be!assumed!that!this!data!has!a!representative!span!of!minimum!10!years.!
It! is! also! taken! into! the! account! that! the! technology! and! infrastructure! is! not! expected! to!
change!considerably!during!the!next!10!years.!!
Technological)representativeness)
The!amount!of!process!technology!data!related!to!metals!is!in!general!very!scarce!and!was!
collected!from!the!best!available!sources.!The!preRtreatment!facility!process!was!modelled!as!
Hischier!et!al.!(2007)!recommended,!where!the!preRtreatment!facility!model!was!based!on!
German! and! Dutch! recycling! companies! [49].! Hence,! it! is! assumed! that! latter! technology!
should!not!differ!significantly!and!is!also!representative!for!Denmark!in!terms!of!emissions!and!
energy!consumption.!On!the!other!hand,!this!data!was!gathered!for!WEEE!in!general,!not!
specifically!for!mobile!phones.!Mobile!phones!can!be!classified!as!small!WEEE!(sRWEEE)!and!
several!studies!have!found!that!performance!of!the!recycling!processes!highly!depends!on!the!
composition! of! WEEE! [13,! 56].! Therefore,! the! preRtreatment! facilities’! recovery! rates! and!
distribution!of!materials!into!the!different!fractions!is!not!entirely!representative!and!might!
yield!the!uncertainty!in!this!study.!Data!for!metallurgy!plant!can!be!considered!representative,!
since!it!is!based!on!Boliden!reports![57],!which!is!the!actual!place!where!the!metal!recovery!
takes!place!in!the!system.!Only!the!first,!smelting!phase!was!based!on!Umicores!process![51].!

9.!Sensitivity!analysis!
Based!on!process!and!substance!contribution!this!LCA!part!is!focusing!on!the!robustness!of!the!
study!by!detecting!the!sensitive!points!of!the!system!and!assessing!the!influence!of!change!in!
relevant!parameters.!The!purpose!of!sensitivity!analysis!is!to!see!how!the!data!and!assumptions!
used!in!the!study!are!reliable.!The!most!significant!issues!of!the!system!that!are!contributing!
the!most!to!the!final!LCIA!results!will!be!assessed!in!terms!of!sensitivity!and!discussed!next.!
Two!types!of!sensitivity!analysis!approaches!are!defined![63]:!!
•!
Sensitivity! of! an! output! parameter! y! to! an! input! parameter! x! connected! through! a!
continuous!function!y!=!f(x),!e.g.!increase!of!10%.!!
•!
Sensitivity!associated!with!a!discrete!choice,!e.g.!system!boundaries,!allocation!rules,!
functional!unit,!impact!method,!etc.!
In!this!study,!the!most!important!assumptions!made!could!be!considered!as!follows:!
•!
External!processes!related!to!virgin!metal!extraction!(substituting!processes)!in!both!
scenarios.!
•!
Material!composition!entering!the!preRtreatment!facility.!InputRspecific!emissions!in!
preRtreatment!facility!in!scenario!1.!!
To!assess!the!sensitivity!by!calculating!the!sensitivity!ratio!of!the!parameters!the!perturbation!
analysis!was!conducted.!The!sensitivity!ratio!(SR)!shows!the!effect!on!the!output!by!a!change!
in!the!input,!e.g.!if!the!sensitivity!ratio!is!2,!it!implies!that!when!increasing!its!value!by!10%,!the!
final!result!is!increased!by!20%.!It!is!expressed!as![64]:!
