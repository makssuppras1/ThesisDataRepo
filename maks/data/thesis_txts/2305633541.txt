ABSTRACT 
Food!packaging!is!an!inevitable!part!of!modern!life!that!enables!the!separation!of!food!
production!and!food!consumption.!Over!the!years!many!innovations!have!made!food!packaging!
cheaper,!smarter,!better!and!more!sustainable.!For!many!years!companies!have!embraced!the!
idea!that!sustainability!can!be!good!business!and!tried!to!achieve!higher!ecoQefficiency!by!
minimising!packaging!and!optimising!production.!In!recent!years!intensifying!climate!change,!
limited!supply!horizons!and!increasing!pressure!on!ecosystems!in!general!have!challenged!the!
current!linear!economy!and!fostered!new!thoughts!about!sustainability!in!a!Circular!Economy.!
The!increasing!environmental!awareness!among!consumers!is!starting!to!incentivize!consumer!
goods!companies!to!come!up!with!new!green!ecoQeffective!packaging!solutions!that!fit!within!
the!new!economic!system.!Such!new!packaging!innovation!should!both!be!sustainable!and!be!
perceived!as!being!sustainable!in!order!to!be!attractive!to!consumers,!but!until!now!there!have!
been!a!gap!between!perceived!and!assessed!sustainability.!The!aim!is!to!answer!how!Danish!
consumers!perceive!the!environmental!sustainability!of!liquid!food!packaging!and!investigate!
how!this!correlates!with!LCA!studies.!The!project!is!done!in!collaboration!with!COOP!Danmark.!
!
The!research!has!two!sides;!a!survey!among!197!Danish!consumers!and!qualitative!interviews!
with!10!Millennials!tries!to!uncover!how!different!packaging!alternatives!are!perceived!and!how!
consumers!reason.!A!literature!reviews!of!Life!Cycle!Assessment!(LCA)!studies!with!in!5!product!
categories!and!a!streamlined!LCA!modelling!of!beer,!soft!drinks!and!olive!oil!tries!to!uncover!
what!can!be!assessed!as!the!most!environmentally!sustainable!packaging!solution.!The!research!
shows!that!consumers!assess!packaging!sustainability!based!on!the!material!type!and!what!they!
can!personally!do!at!the!disposal!stage.!Consumers!do!in!general!not!consider!the!impact!of!
production!and!transport.!New!bioQbased!packaging!types!and!glass!packaging!is!perceived!the!
most!sustainable!and!plastic!in!general!have!a!bad!perception.!Laminated!cartons!have!a!more!
mixed!reputation.!The!results!from!the!LCA!literature!review!show!that!this!is!problematic!since!
plastic!and!especially!laminated!cartons!can!be!very!sustainable!solutions,!even!though!they!are!
difficult!to!recycle.!The!LCA!study!also!showed!that!there!is!a!big!environmental!difference!
between!glass!being!reused!and!recycled,!but!consumers!are!not!aware!of!this!difference.!!
!
A!group!of!23!sustainability!experts!who!work!professionally!with!sustainability!assessment!also!
answered!the!survey.!Contradictory!to!expected!it!was!found!that!they!perceived!glass!more!
environmentally!friendly!than!consumers.!The!experts!were!also!more!sceptical!to!bioQbased!
solutions.!
!
In!the!end!the!implications!of!the!various!findings!are!discussed!and!strategic!advice!is!given!that!
can!guide!future!green!packaging!initiatives!in!Danish!supermarkets.!

1 INTRODUCTION 
In!September!2015!Arla!Foods!launched!a!new!milk!packaging!for!the!Danish!market!(Arla!Foods!2015).!
The!form!factor!was!still!the!same,!but!compared!to!the!earlier!version,!the!graphic!had!changed!and!
the!packaging!now!had!a!brown!recycledQpaperQlook.!After!purchase!the!vigilant!consumer!would!
quickly!realise!that!the!recycledQlook!was!only!superficial,!since!the!bottom!was!still!the!same!white!as!
before.!Is!this!another!example!of!Green!Washing!where!companies!make!products!that!appear!to!be!
green!and!sustainable,!but!are!only!so!on!the!surface?!The!new!colour!and!graphics!were!chosen!by!the!
marketing!department!to!signal!that!this!was!now!a!more!sustainable!packaging,!achieved!not!by!using!
recycled!paper,!but!instead!by!using!sustainably!sourced!FSC!certified!wood!and!plastic!from!renewable!
resources.!Arla!claims!that!20%!less!C02!is!emitted!from!the!new!packaging!compared!to!conventional!
milk!cartons!(Arla!Foods,!2015).!!
With!the!new!packaging!Arla!takes!part!in!a!trend!where!new!environmentally!friendly!packaging!is!
developed!and!marketed!as!a!way!to!differentiate!and!gain!competitive!advantage!as!a!sustainable!
brand.!Arla!follows!other!big!fastQmovingQconsumerQgoods!(FMCG)!companies.!To!give!a!few!examples:!
Unilever!is!working!rigorously!on!packaging!light!weighting!and!increasing!the!amount!of!recycled!
content!and!have!recently!introduced!refillable!soap!packaging!in!several!markets!(Unilever!2014),!in!
2009!CocaQCola!marketed!their!Plant&Bottle!with!up!to!30%!plastic!from!renewable!sources!(The!CocaQ
Cola!Company!2015)!and!at!the!moment!Carlsberg!Group!is!developing!the!world’s!first!beer!bottle!
made!from!wood!fibre!(Carlsberg!Group!2015).!Are!the!examples!a!sign!of!real!and!serious!
environmental!commitment!or!a!temporary!and!superficial!pursuit!to!gain!market!share!and!buy!
goodwill!with!superficial!green!efforts!with!limited!impact?!There!is!no!easy!answer,!as!the!conclusion!
depends!on!how!sustainability!is!valued,!defined!and!assessed.!Sustainability!assessment!is!a!complex!
subject!that!requires!a!lot!of!data!and!interdisciplinary!knowledge.!As!a!consumer!it!can!therefore!be!
challenging!to!evaluate!the!different!products!offered!in!the!Supermarket!and!see!through!the!claims!
of!large!consumer!companies.!!
!
Packaging!waste!is!a!very!tangible!and!visible!aspect!of!everyday!consumption!and!has!therefore!got!a!
lot!of!attention!from!consumers,!politicians,!businesses!and!academics!over!the!years.!The!very!first!
Life!Cycle!Assessment!(LCA)!studies!was!done!on!beverage!packaging!(Hunt!&!Franklin!1996)!and!over!
the!years!many!LCA!studies!have!tried!to!answer!what!the!most!sustainable!packaging!option!is.!It!is!
difficult!to!come!up!with!a!general!answer!to!this!question!as!the!conclusion!is!highly!context!
dependent!and!rests!on!the!local!infrastructure!and!recycling!system.!!
!
For!many!products,!such!as!beverages,!packaging!is!a!necessity.!As!consumers!in!general!put!greater!
emphasis!on!environmental!issues,!and!FMCGQcompanies!invest!in!positioning!themselves!as!green!and!
sustainable,!it!is!interesting!to!ask!what!consumers!perceive!as!being!the!most!sustainable!packaging!
option.!A!few!studies!have!looked!into!perception!of!packaging!materials!(van!Dam!&!van!Trijp!1994;!
van!Dam!1996;!Lindh!et!al.!2015;!Korhonen!et!al.!2015)!but!no!studies!have!fully!compared!perceived!
sustainability!with!results!from!LCA!studies.!Doing!so!would!provide!valuable!knowledge!that!could!be!
used!for!better!information!to!consumers!and!sustainable!packaging!design!that!are!aligned!with!
consumers!expectations!and!therefore!have!a!much!better!chance!for!success!in!the!market.!!

1.2 Scope and limitations 
The!thesis!has!a!multidisciplinary!approach!that!combines!insights!from!different!scientific!fields.!The!
three!main!areas!are!illustrated!in!figure!1Q2.!
!
The!scope!of!this!thesis!is!limited!to!environmental!sustainability!for!primary!food!packaging!for!liquids!
on!the!Danish!market.!There!are!several!reasons!for!this.!Food!packaging!is!the!largest!packaging!
category!so!the!impact!is!considerable!(Verghese!et!al.!2012).!The!focus!on!liquids!is!due!to!the!fact!
that!packaging!is!necessary!for!these!products,!so!consumers!
won’t!question!if!packaging!is!necessary!or!not,!and!focus!can!
be!directed!to!how!different!packaging!alternatives!are!in!fact!
perceived.!Focusing!on!packaging!for!liquids!also!allows!for!a!
better!comparison!across!different!product!categories,!than!a!
broader!focus!on!food!packaging!in!general!would!do.!Another!
reason!is!that!the!relative!environmental!importance!of!the!
packaging!compared!to!the!content!is!much!higher!for!
beverages!and!liquid!food!products!and!beverages!than!for!
other!types!of!food!Q!for!soft!drinks!the!packaging!accounts!for!
more!than!50%!of!the!overall!carbon!footprint!compared!to!
10%!for!food!in!general!(INCPEN!2009).!In!the!report!packaging!
refers!to!food!packaging,!if!not!stated!otherwise.!
!
The!geographical!scope!of!the!data!collection!was!limited!to!
Denmark.!This!was!done!for!two!reasons;!Firstly!COOP!
Danmark!is!a!major!stakeholder!in!the!project!and!they!are!mostly!interested!in!result!from!Danish!
consumers.!Secondly!a!large!personal!network!in!Denmark!meant!easier!access!to!respondents.!!
!
Packaging!is!more!than!what!meets!the!eye!of!the!consumer.!The!whole!packaging!system!consists!of!
primary,!secondary!and!tertiary!packaging.!Primary!packaging!is!what!contains!the!content.!Secondary!
packaging!is!used!for!handling!of!more!than!one!product!and!for!store!display.!Cardboard!boxes!and!
beer!crates!are!examples!of!this.!Tertiary!packaging!is!used!for!transport!and!never!seen!by!the!
consumer.!Since!secondary!and!tertiary!packaging!is!kept!in!a!more!closed!businessQtoQbusiness!
environment,!the!recycling!rates!and!the!quality!of!the!recycled!content!are!much!higher!for!these!
types!of!packaging!(Verghese!et!al.!2012).!Therefore!the!environmental!impact!from!secondary!and!
tertiary!are!smaller!and!in!this!report!the!main!focus!will!be!on!primary!packaging.!!

1.6 Structure of the report 
The!thesis!has!four!main!sections.!!!
The!first!introductory!part!sets!the!frame.!The!role!of!packaging!in!society!in!the!past!and!present!is!

2.1 The evolution of packaging and society 
Food!packaging!is!not!a!new!invention.!It!dates!back!to!the!very!beginning!of!civilisation.!When!humans!
began!to!cultivate!land!and!have!livestock,!a!need!emerged!to!be!able!to!contain,!transport!and!
exchange!produced!food.!Since!ancient!times!pots,!jars,!barrels!and!sacks!have!served!the!purpose,!as!
well!as!“singleQuse”!packaging!made!of!leaves!and!other!natural!materials,!the!former!being!reusable!
and!the!latter!compostable.!
!
Packaging!waste!however!is!a!relatively!new!problem!and!a!product!of!the!industrialised!society.!100!
years!ago!the!majority!of!the!population!in!the!Western!world!lived!on!the!countryside.!This!meant!
that!a!large!proportion!of!food!was!grown!locally,!bought!loose!and!prepared!in!the!home,!without!
further!processing.!Packaging!was!generally!owned!by!the!consumer,!and!almost!entirely!reused!(Ellen!
MacArthur!Foundation!2013b).!All!these!factors!meant!that!the!environmental!impact!of!packaging!
was!minimised.!In!Denmark!it!was!not!mandatory!to!take!part!in!municipal!waste!collection!until!the!
1970’s,!so!on!the!countryside!it!was!common!to!reuse!and!recycle!most!packaging!and!burn!the!rest!
locally.!!
!
In!the!first!half!of!the!previous!century!food!production!and!food!consumption!to!a!large!degree!
became!separated,!as!farming!became!industrialised!and!many!people!moved!to!the!larger!cities.!This!
created!new!requirements!for!packaging!that!could!contain,!preserve!and!sell!food!away!from!where!it!
was!being!produced.!Since!then!the!use!of!packaging!has!been!increasing!(Verghese!et!al.!2012).!

industrial!revolution:!Design!for!production,!Design!for!selling,!Design!for!quality!and!Design!for!
usability.!The!development!of!food!packaging!in!the!last!200!years!will!now!be!explained!using!these!
four!roles!and!by!highlighting!important!packaging!innovations.!!

2.2.1 Positive effects of packaging 
Contrary!to!popular!belief,!packaging!can!have!positive!effects!on!the!environment,!since!it!enables!
safe!and!efficient!supply!of!products!and!it!minimises!the!environmental!impacts!of!producing,!
transporting,!using!and!disposing!of!products!(Verghese!et!al.!2012)!!
The!most!important!quality!is!that!packaging!prolongs!the!lifetime!of!the!food!it!contains!and!thereby!
minimises!food!waste.!The!environmental!footprint!of!the!food!is!in!general!much!higher!than!the!
negative!impact!of!the!packaging,!so!if!the!avoided!emissions!from!the!food!that!the!packaging!saves!
exceed!the!emissions!from!the!packaging!itself,!then!the!net!result!is!actually!positive.!This!means!that!
more!packaging!can!actually!be!a!good!thing.!Therefore!packaging!optimisation!is!often!better!than!
packaging!minimisation!as!it!is!important!to!have!the!right!amount!of!packaging!(see!figure!2Q2)!
!
Using!more!packaging!material!will!usually!
provide!better!mechanical!protection!and!thereby!
fewer!broken!packages!that!need!to!be!discarded!
before!the!content!is!consumed.!But!it!is!not!just!
the!amount!of!packaging!material!used!or!the!
inherent!conserving!qualities!of!the!materials!that!
prevent!food!waste.!How!the!functionality!of!the!
packaging!take!use!into!account!is!very!important!
in!order!to!minimise!food!waste!(see!section!
2.4.3)!
!
If!packaging!significantly!leads!to!a!decrease!in!
food!waste!this!should!also!be!considered!in!
packaging!LCA!studies.!This!requires!an!“up!
scaling”!of!the!functional!unit!to!become!the!delivery!of!eaten!food!instead!of!just!the!packaging!itself!
(Wikström!et!al.!2014).!This!is!especially!the!case!if!the!packaging!contains!food!with!a!high!
environmental!impact!or!the!packaging!are!compared!with!solutions!without!packaging,!since!
packaging!in!general!decreases!food!waste!and!the!difference!between!different!packaging!types!is!not!
as!big!as!packaging!versus!no!packaging!is!in!general.!!
!
The!relative!impact!of!packaging!compared!to!the!food!it!contains!differs!a!lot!for!different!food!
categories,!since!some!kinds!of!food,!such!as!meat,!have!a!much!larger!environmental!footprint!than!
others.!On!average!packaging!accounts!for!5Q7%!of!the!energy!related!to!weekly!food!consumption!
(Coles!&!Kirwan!2011;!INCPEN!2009).!The!food!supply!itself!accounts!for!50%!and!the!rest!goes!to!
cooking,!refrigeration!and!transport!(INCPEN!2009).!Packaging’s!relative!contribution!is!larger!for!
beverages.!This!is!especially!the!case!for!alcoholic!beverages!and!soft!drinks:!Soft!drinks!are!the!only!
food!group!where!it!takes!more!energy!to!make!the!packaging!(46%)!than!the!product!(26%)!(INCPEN!
2009).!The!amount!of!food!waste!is!relatively!small!for!beverages!and!liquid!foods!and!therefore!food!
waste!is!not!considered!in!this!study.!
!
Even!though!the!environmental!impact!(measured!in!energy)!that!is!used!for!packaging!is!small!
compared!to!other!human!activities,!there!are!good!reasons!to!develop!more!sustainable!solutions!and!
these!drivers!will!now!be!elaborated.!!

2.6 Subconclusion 
Packaging!has!existed!since!the!dawn!of!civilisation,!but!packaging!waste!is!a!product!of!our!modern!
industrialised!society.!Today!packaging!is!a!very!visible!aspect!of!everyday!consumption!and!therefore!

!

3.1 Life cycle assessment 
The!rising!environmental!concern!in!the!1960’s!and!early!1970’s!created!interest!among!scholars!on!
how!to!measure!and!assess!sustainability.!Packaging!was!one!of!the!first!subjects!of!interest.!For!many!
years!there!have!been!scientific!consensus!that!packaging!should!be!assessed!in!a!life!cycle!
perspective.!The!methodology!for!such!Life!Cycle!Assessment!(LCA)!have!been!developed!and!refined!
for!more!than!45!years!and!is!now!standardised!internationally!in!ISO!14040!and!ISO!14044.!Today!“Life&
Cycle&Thinking&and&Life&Cycle&Assessment&(LCA)&are&the&scientific&approaches&behind&modern&
environmental&policies&and&business&decision&support&related&to&Sustainable&Consumption&and&
Production.”!(European!Commission!2010). Results!from!a!Life!Cycle!Impact!Assessment!(LCIA)!reflects!
the!whole!product!life!cycle!and!are!therefore!preferable,!compared!to!single!attributes!such!as!
recycled!content!that!can!be!misleading!and!simply!move!the!impact!from!one!part!of!the!life!cycle!to!
another!(UNEP!&!SETAC!2013).!!
!
Recently!new!methods!have!been!proposed!to!simplify!the!LCA!process!to!make!it!easier!for!companies!
(Vogtlander!2014),!to!relate!it!to!planetary!boundaries!(Bjørn!&!Hauschild!2015)!and!make!it!more!
useful!in!the!context!of!a!circular!economy!(Ellen!MacArthur!Foundation!2015).!In!this!chapter!the!
concept!of!LCA!will!be!explained,!with!special!emphasis!on!the!process!of!normalisation!and!weighting!
in!LCIA!and!with!discussion!of!how!to!measure!reuse!and!recyclability.!Alternative!methods!for!
normalisation!and!weighting,!such!as!the!ecoQcost!method!and!carrying!capacityQbased!methods!will!
also!briefly!be!discussed.!
3.2 Life cycle assessment procedure 
LCA!can!be!used!in!different!ways,!from!fast!evaluation!of!alternative!product!ideas!at!an!early!
conceptual!stage!to!inQdepth!monitoring!of!production!processes.!The!purpose!requires!different!levels!
of!complexity!and!detail.!LCA’s!can!be!divided!in!three!groups!according!to!their!complexity:!(Verghese!
et!al.!2012)!
!
• 
Conceptual!LCA!–!fast,!highQlevel!analysis!of!the!main!processes!in!the!life!cycle.!Sometimes!
referred!to!as!Life!cycle!thinking.!Time!needed:!hours.!!
• 
Streamlined!LCA!–!shortened!form!of!LCA!for!gaining!insights!and!identifying!focus!areas!in!
internal!development!processes!(not!external!communication).!Time!needed:!days!to!weeks!
• 
Full!LCA!–!Detailed!and!complete!form!of!LCA!compliant!with!international!standards.!Results!
can!be!communicated!externally.!Time!needed:!weeks!to!years.!
!
In!a!full!LCA!all!stages!of!the!product!system!is!evaluated!thoroughly!according!to!the!standards!in!ISO!
14040!and!ISO!14044.!!According!to!ISO!an!LCA!is:!“the&compilation&and&evaluation&of&the&inputs,&
outputs,&and&the&potential&environmental&impacts&of&a&product&system,&scaled&to&a&functional&unit,&
throughout&its&life&cycle.”&(ISO&14044)!

2. Life!cycle!inventory!analysis!!

the!impact!they!each!have!on!the!environment,!so!the!LCI!can!be!transformed!into!impact!scores!for!a!
limited!number!of!impact!categories.!This!is!done!by!classifying!the!relevant!emissions!and!then!make!a!
characterisation!based!on!their!environmental!burden,!which!results!in!a!score!for!each!impact!
category.!Different!impact!assessment!methods!have!been!developed,!for!example!ReCiPe,!EcoQ
Indicator!99,!CML!2001!and!IMPACT!2002+.!The!characterised!impact!scores!can!be!further!normalised!
and!weighted!to!give!an!overall!score,!which!enables!comparison!but!also!introduces!a!lot!of!
uncertainty.!These!issues!will!be!discussed!more!in!depth!in!the!next!paragraph.!!!
3.2.4 4. Interpretation 
The!last!stage!is!interpretation!of!the!results!and!evaluation!according!to!the!goal!of!the!study.!Where!
are!the!largest!environmental!impacts?!What!are!the!consequences!of!the!results?!Uncertainty!and!
sensitivity!of!the!results!should!be!considered.!!

3.3.1 1. Classification 
In!the!classification!step!the!relevant!impact!categories!are!chosen!and!each!of!the!emissions!in!the!LCI!
are!assigned!to!one!or!several!impact!categories.!Usually!a!standard!set!of!impact!categories!are!used.!
Commonly!used!impact!categories!for!packaging!are:!global!warming,!stratospheric!ozone!depletion,!
acidification,!eutrophication,!photochemical!smog,!terrestrial!toxicity,!aquatic!toxicity,!human!health,!
resource!depletion,!land!use!and!water!use!(Verghese!et!al.!2012).!It!is!important!to!choose!the!right!
impact!categories,!especially!if!the!study!is!done!on!the!basis!of!a!custom!LCI!–!to!few!means!that!
important!environmental!impacts!could!be!ignored!and!to!many!cost!time!and!money.!Even!with!many!
impact!categories!it!is!important!to!remember!that!not!everything!is!easy!to!measure!in!an!LCA.!
Littering!and!the!impact!on!animal!and!plants!is!an!example!of!this.!For!this!reason!LCAs&“need&to&be&
considered&in&conjunction&with&other&sources&of&environmental&information&and&packaging&specific&
sustainability&metrics&to&support&packaging&decision6making”&(Verghese!et!al.!2012).!
!
3.3.2 2. Characterisation 
In!the!characterisation!stage!each!of!the!emissions!in!the!LCI!are!transformed!to!impact!categories!with!
a!common!unit.!A!characterisation!factor,!that!expresses!the!specific!impact,!is!derived.!An!example!of!
this!is!that!global!warming!potential!is!measured!in!kg!CO2!equivalent!(CO2e),!meaning!that!other!green!
house!gasses!such!as!methane!is!translated!into!tons!of!CO2!with!a!characterisation!factor!according!to!
their!contribution!to!global!warming!relative!to!CO2!(for!methane!this!factor!is!21).!Results!for!the!
impact!categories!can!be!found!at!midpoint!or!endpoint!levels.!The!impacts!may!happen!at!very!
different!points!in!time!–!some!at!the!production!stage,!others!after!many!years!in!a!landfill.!The!
“impact!pathway”!should!therefore!be!considered.!Some!impacts!are!global!(such!as!global!warming!
potential)!and!others!are!local.!For!the!latter!the!impact!can!differ!dependent!on!geography.!The!
spatial!and!temporal!conditions!of!a!production!system!are!usually!poorly!resolved!(Hauschild!2005).!
3.3.3 3. Normalisation 
It!is!rare!that!an!alternative!is!better!in!all!aspects!and!tradeQoffs!between!the!categories!should!
therefore!be!evaluated,!which!is!possible!with!normalisation.!In!the!normalisation!stage!the!different!
impact!indicators!are!translated!to!a!common!scale!that!allow!for!comparison!of!the!results!in!the!
impact!categories!and!provides!information!on!where!it!is!most!important!to!focus.!The!impact!scores!
are!related!to!a!common!reference,!often!one!person’s!relative!share!of!society’s!overall!annual!
impact.!The!unit!is!milliQpersonQequivalent!(mPE)!and!one!PE!then!equals!one!person’s!total!annual!
impact!(Hauschild!2005).!!
Recently!work!has!been!done!to!use!earth’s!total!carrying!capacity!as!the!basis!for!normalisation!in!LCA!
studies!(Bjørn!&!Hauschild!2015).!The!idea!is!that!there!are!critical!threshold!levels!for!all!
environmental!impacts!after!which!the!negative!changes!will!be!difficult!or!impossible!to!revert.!This!
global!and!holistic!approach!is!termed!absolute&sustainability.!The!work!by!Bjørn!and!Hauschild!(2015)!
proposes!normalisation!references!that!expresses!the!personal!share!each!person!have!annually!in!
order!to!keep!within!Earth’s!carrying&capacity.!The!research!is!still!at!an!early!stage!and!unresolved!
questions!remain!with!regards!to!issues!such!as!handling!the!spatial!variation!for!impact!categories!
that!function!on!a!local!or!regional!scale.!

3.4.2 Circularity indicators 
In!2015!The!Ellen!MacArthur!Foundation!introduced!a!new!sustainability!metric;!Material&Circularity&
Indicator&(MCI)!that!expresses!material!circularity&on!a&product&or&company&level,!by!focusing!on!the!
restoration!of!material!flows!throughout!the!life!cycle.!This!is!done!by!considering!the!degree!to!which;!
(A)!Feedstock!comes!from!reused!of!recycled!sources;!(B)!Components!are!reused!or!materials!are!
recycled!after!the!use!of!the!product;!(C)!Products!are!used!longer;!(D)!Products!are!used!more!
intensively!(via!service!or!performance!models)!
Due!to!the!short!lifetime!of!packaging!the!first!two!points!appear!to!have!the!largest!relevance,!but!
considering!the!environmental!benefits!of!the!inner!reuse!loop!(see!figure!2.7)!may!open!up!for!new!
innovative!packaging!systems!that!pushes!the!distinction!between!packaging!and!product.!At!the!
moment!the!Circularity!Indicator!methodology!only!includes!the!technical&sphere!and!is!therefore!not!
suited!for!calculating!the!positive!effects!of!bioQbased!or!biodegradable!materials!(Ellen!MacArthur!
Foundation!2015).!In!comparison!to!LCA,!the!MCI!approach!recognises!utility!of!the!product!(durability!
and!usage!intensity)!and!focuses!on!materials!flows.!The!MCI!does!not!express!direct!environmental!
impact!and!is!not!contradictory!to!LCA,!but!should!be!seen!as!complimentary.!
!
3.5 Subconclusion 
Sustainability!is!a!complex!issue!and!it!is!not!easy!to!attach!a!single!measure!that!fully!reflects!a!
product’s!environmental!sustainability.!Damage!based!weighting!is!a!method!to!come!up!with!a!single!
answer,!but!the!weighting,!is!complex!and!nonQstandardised,!which!makes!the!results!nonQtransparent,!
prone!to!subjective!opinions!and!difficult!to!compare.!Therefore!a!wide!number!of!midQpoint!impacts!
are!normally!shown!in!LCA!studies!instead.!This!is!much!more!transparent!since!the!characterisation!
step!and!classification!steps!are!standardised,!but!difficult!to!relate!to!overall!environmental!impacts.!A!
preventionQbased!method,!such!as!the!ecoQcost!method,!is!a!promising!alternative.!Carbon!footprint!is!
the!most!used!singleQissue!indicator,!and!even!though!it!is!connected!to!other!indicators,!it!is!not!
related!to!important!aspects!of!environmental!sustainability!such!as!the!effect!on!human!health.!In!
current!LCA’s!recycling!is!not!always!accounted!for!in!the!same!way!and!new!ways!of!measuring!
circularity,!such!as!the!new!Material!Circularity!Indicator,!could!be!a!good!supplement!to!existing!LCA!
methodology.!

and!behaviours.!At!first!general!attitudes!to!sustainability!will!be!investigated!and!then!it!will!be!

4.2 The ethical consumer  
The!90's!saw!the!rise!of!the!political!consumer!Q!the!engaged!and!responsible!citizen!that!voted!for!a!
better!world!through!their!purchases!in!the!supermarket.!Organic!and!FairQtrade!products!were!
introduced!to!the!market!and!became!popular.!This!“political!consumerism”!can!be!seen!as!a!shift!of!
power!from!government!to!the!individual,!possibly!as!a!response!to!the!struggle!of!governments!to!
tackle!large!environmental!problems!such!as!climate!change!(Stolle!et!al.!2005).!Purchasing!sustainable!
products!can!therefore!be!seen!as!micro!political!acts!that!represent!personal!values.!This!aligns!with!
the!view!that!environmental!attitudes!have!changed!from!an!economic!to!a!moral!orientation!
(Thøgersen!1999).!Another!view!is!that!green!consumption!has!become!a!lifestyle!marker!and!that!
sustainable!purchases!are!instrumental!in!obtaining!social!status!(Gilg!et!al.!2005).!It!should!be!pointed!
out!that!these!two!motives!are!not!necessarily!conflicting.!!
!
Research!shows!that!when!consumers!are!asked!directly!they!show!a!high!level!of!concern!for!the!
environment.!People!also!tend!to!think!that!it!is!a!personal!responsibility!to!contribute!to!a!more!
sustainable!world!through!personal!actions!–!including!the!products!they!buy.!In!a!large!UK!study!81%!
of!consumers!stated!that!they!think!about!the!environmental!impact!when!buying!groceries!(Martin!
2010).!Sanna!&!Klocker!(2015)!measured!consumers’!level!of!environmental!concern!based!on!their!
rationalisations!after!a!purchase!and!concluded!that!36%!was!concerned!and!43%!showed!limited!
concern,!which!could!point!out!that!consumers!overstate!their!environmental!concern!when!asked!
directly,!compared!to!what!happens!in!the!purchasing!situation.!
!

4.4 Insights from behavioural science 
The!fact!that!good!intentions!and!environmental!concerns!don’t!necessarily!translate!into!action!can!be!
explained!with!the!Theory!of!Planned!Behaviour!(Ajzen!1991),!which!says!that!behaviour!is!the!result!of!
intentions!and!intentions!are!formed!by!attitudes,!perceived!behavioural!control!and!the!social!norm!
(see!figure!4.1).!Empirical!studies!have!shown!that!the!link!between!attitudes,!perceived!control,!
beliefs!and!intentions!are!strong,!but!that!the!link!between!intentions!and!behaviour!is!weak!(Polizzi!Di!
Sorrentino!et!al.!2016).!There!is!a!soQcalled!“attitudeQbehavioural!gap”!(Carrigan!&!Attala!2001)!Q!!a!
difference!between!what!people!intend!to!do!and!what!they!actually!do!in!the!supermarket.!Others!
point!out!that!behaviour!is!determined!not!only!by!motivation!but!that!ability!and!possibility!are!
equally!important!(Thøgersen!1992).!Ability!in!this!sense!refers!to!individual!abilities!(knowledge,!
personal!finances,!etc.)!and!opportunities!to!the!outer!circumstances!such!as!availability.!!

4.5 Profile of the pro-environmental consumer 
From!various!studies!a!profile!of!the!environmentally!conscious!consumer!can!be!drawn:!highly!
educated,!relatively!wealthy,!small!household!size,!young!and!female!(Carrigan!&!Attala!2001;!Stolle!et!
al.!2005;!Martin!2010).!Overall!this!is!backed!by!the!Deloitte!study,!except!for!the!fact!that!they!
actually!found!the!typical!green!consumer!to!be!older!than!the!average.!They!note!that!young!people!
have!a!more!positive!attitude!to!green!consumption,!but!that!the!attitudeQbehavioural!gap!is!larger,!so!
young!people!want!to!buy!green!products,!but!doesn’t!do!so,!most!likely!due!to!lower!disposable!
income.!The!Deloitte!study!also!points!out!that!there!is!a!big!spread!along!all!income!ranges,!age!
brackets,!education!levels!and!various!household!sizes,!so!it!is!difficult!to!generalize!about!the!green!
consumer.!Rokka!&!Uusitalo!(2008)!state!that!according!to!their!study!based!on!a!conjoint!choice!
analysis,!rather!than!an!attitude!survey,!it!is!difficult!to!make!any!conclusions!on!which!demographic!

4.6 Perception of packaging  
Many!studies!have!been!done!on!the!perception!of!packaging!from!a!marketing!perspective.!
Ampuero!&!Vila!(2006)!studied!consumers’!perception!of!different!colours,!fonts!and!shapes!on!
packaging!and!mapped!how!the!different!graphical!appearances!lead!to!different!positioning!in!the!
market.!The!conclusions!from!the!empirical!study!confirmed!what!is!regarded!as!normative!knowledge!
in!the!graphic!design!community.!GeliciQZeko!et!al.!(2013)!studied!perception!of!milk!packaging!and!
found!that!when!investigating!consumer!perceptions!it!is!not!sufficient!to!categorise!in!predefined!
categories,!but!preferable!with!a!more!open!approach!where!the!consumers!articulate!their!own!
perceptions!which!can!be!plotted!into!perceptual!maps.!
!
It!is!important!to!note!that!many!external!factors!influence!the!perception!of!a!product!other!than!the!
packaging,!including!the!purchasing!situation,!branding,!advertisement!and!the!consumers’!attitude!to!
the!other!characteristics!of!the!product!(such!as!a!taste!and!nutrition).!!
!
It!is!interesting!to!note!that!consumers!can!evaluate!the!functional!qualities!of!a!packaging!when!they!
use!it,!but!have!to!rely!on!external!sources!of!information!for!the!environmental!effects!of!the!

(BechQLarsen!1996).!Some!environmental!qualities!are!difficult!for!consumers!to!access!and!some!are!
completely!impossible!to!assess.!An!example!of!the!latter!is!the!statement!that!a!product!is!100%!PVC!
free.!The!packaging!characteristics!that!consumers!can!actually!evaluate!can!be!divided!into!intrinsic!
factors!(size,!form!and!material!type)!and!system!factors!(how!well!the!products!characteristics!fit!with!
the!local!system!for!reuse!and!recycling).!!!

4.9 Labelling and product information 
Packaging!material!is!only!one!aspect!that!influences!consumers’!perception.!The!graphic!design,!
product!information!and!labels!are!other!important!aspects!that!will!now!be!considered.!!
!
First!of!all!it!can!be!concluded!that!labels!have!an!effect.!A!British!study!(Martin!2010)!shows!that!labels!
and!claims!directly!on!the!packaging!are!more!likely!to!convince!consumers!about!the!sincerity!of!a!
company’s!environmental!efforts,!than!advertisement!or!corporate!pledges.!70%!of!consumers!state!
that!they!are!very!likely!or!somewhat!likely!to!be!convinced!by!packaging!and!labelling.!The!study!also!

women!would!be!somewhat!likely!or!very!likely!to!be!convinced!by!packaging,!compared!to!62%!for!
men.!For!labels!the!numbers!are!19%!for!women!and!7%!for!men!(Martin!2010).!A!study!by!BechQ

that!help!consumers,!but!on!the!other!hand!also!can!misguide!consumers!and!in!more!extreme!cases!

4.10 Subconclusion 
It!can!be!concluded!that!consumers!in!general!are!positive!to!environmentally!friendly!products,!but!
that!a!positive!attitude!doesn’t!automatically!translate!into!action.!When!asked!in!general!packaging!is!
seen!as!something!negative,!and!the!positive!impact!of!food!preservation!is!overlooked.!An!important!

importance,!but!the!number!of!ethically!concerned!consumers!is!increasing.!!

6.2.3 Considerations in selecting the packaging alternatives 
In!life!cycle!assessment!it!is!important!to!define!the!functional!unit!in!order!to!be!able!to!compare!
different!alternatives.!The!products!have!to!perform!the!same!function!to!be!comparable,!but!defining!
this!functionality!can!be!challenging!and!should!be!done!carefully!(Hauschild!2005).!If!the!definition!is!
very!narrow!and!specific!some!solutions!might!be!left!out,!whereas!a!more!open!and!loosely!defined!
functional!unit!might!compare!products!that!have!very!little!in!common.!To!give!an!example,!it!was!

7.1 Introduction 
In!this!chapter!we!will!qualify!the!most!sustainable!solution!within!each!of!the!5!product!categories.!
The!procedure!for!the!systematic!literature!search!was!described!in!the!methodology!section.!If!
nothing!else!is!stated,!it!is!assumed!that!the!evaluated!packaging!alternatives!has!the!same!effect!on!
product!shelf!time,!are!used!the!same!way,!and!lead!to!the!same!amount!of!product!waste.!
7.1.1 The difficulties of generalising 
In!2013!the!United!Nations!Environment!Programme!(UNEP)!published!a!big!report!that!reviewed!69!
scientific!LCA!studies!of!beverage!and!food!packaging.!The!conclusion!was:!“There&are&few,&if&any,&
generalities&about&what&makes&a&package&environmentally&preferable&in&terms&of&materials&or&design&
attributes”!(UNEP!&!SETAC!2013).!So!it!is!not!easy!to!say!what!the!best!packaging!solution!is.!Some!of!
the!difficulties!have!been!described!in!the!LCA!theory!section!(chapter!3)!and!are!due!to!different!LCIA!
modelling!techniques.!But!also!unweighted!results!are!difficult!to!compare.!This!is!due!to!the!fact!that!
LCA!results!are!context!dependent!and!they!strongly!depend!on!the!characteristics!of!the!packaging!
system.!Therefore!“it&is&not&possible&to&identify&a&single&best&material&for&all&packaging&applications&or&a&
single&best&disposal&pathway&for&a&given&material”!(UNEP!&!SETAC!2013).!This!is!not!the!same!as!
concluding!that!LCA’s!are!useless,!but!it!clearly!highlights!the!importance!of!being!aware!of!the!specific!
system!boundaries,!functional!units!and!general!assumptions!when!comparing!different!studies.!It!is!
also!important!to!remember!that!LCA!studies!are!time!specific.!As!the!energy!mix,!legislation!and!
wasteQmanagement!systems!change!over!time,!so!does!the!validity!of!the!conclusions.!!
 
According!to!Verghese!et!al.!(2012)!the!differences!in!the!results!of!the!same!packaging!type!can!be!
attributed!to!differences!in:!the!purpose!of!the!study,!the!defined!functional!unit,!the!data!used!and!
the!defined!system!boundary.!In!the!specific!studies!here!the!four!major!reasons!for!the!variations!are!
1. Different!system!boundaries;!e.g.!some!of!the!studies!include!the!energy!from!cooling!at!home,!
while!others!don’t.!It!is!not!always!clear!if!cooling!is!included!or!not.!!
2. Different!amounts!of!recycled!content.!There!are!big!differences!in!the!amount!of!recycled!
content!in!aluminium!and!glass.!Especially!for!aluminium,!the!amount!of!recycled!content!has!a!
large!effect!on!the!results!
3. Different!endUofUlife!scenarios.!What!happens!with!your!beverage!container!after!consumption,!is!
very!much!dependent!on!where!you!live.!Many!of!the!studies!on!beverage!containers!are!from!the!
UK,!where!beverage!containers!are!either!landfilled!of!recycled.!In!countries!with!refund!systems,!
like!Denmark!and!Germany!there!are!much!larger!degree!of!reuse!and!recycling.!But!even!the!
benefits!from!reusing!vary!according!to!infrastructure!and!geography,!which!will!be!elaborated!in!
the!following!section.!!
4. Different!modelling!assumptions,!both!how!recycling!is!modelled!and!the!LCI!modelling!
(attributional!vs.!consequential)!
!
Pasqualino!et!al.!(2011)!did!a!big!comparative!study!on!different!packaging!materials!and!he!could!
conclude!that!two!factors!are!really!important!when!it!comes!to!determining!packaging!sustainability;!
transport!(of!both!the!packaged!product!and!the!packaging!to!the!final!disposal!destination)!and!use!of!
secondary!materials.!It!is!stated;!“no&conclusions&should&be&drawn&as&to&which&type&of&packaging&has&a&
lower&environmental&impact&unless&these&aspects&are&also&taken&into&consideration.”!
