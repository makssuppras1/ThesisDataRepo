1 INTRODUCTION&&
Clean!drinking!water!is!a!vital!resource!for!all!life!on!earth!and!a!prerequisite!for!human!
health! and! wellness.! Groundwater! and! surface! water! are! primary! sources! used! in! the!
production!of!drinking!water!worldwide!(UN,!2015).!Drinking!water!supply!depends!on!the!
distribution!and!availability!of!the!natural!groundwater!and!surface!water!and!thus!varies!
slightly! between! countries.! In! Denmark,! potable! water! is! exclusively! derived! from!
groundwater!(IWA,!2012).!Groundwater!sites!are!usually!anaerobic!environments,!which!are!
formed! by! rain! infiltrating! the! soil! and! subsurface! (GEUS,! 2015).! The! hydrochemical!
composition!of!groundwater!is!therefore!highly!dependent!on!the!geological!formations!and!
the!flow!rate!of!water,!but!is!also!influenced!by!eventual!solutes!in!the!atmosphere!from!
human! activities! (Søgaard! &! Madsen,! 2013).! The! main! compounds! found! in! Danish!
groundwater!are!shown!in!Table!1!(Søgaard!&!Madsen,!2013).!Many!of!these!chemicals!are!
naturally! occurring,! while! the! presence! of! sulphur! oxides! (SOx)! and! nitrogen! oxides! (NOx)!
gases! and! ammonium! (NH4
+)! is! caused! by! improper! disposal! of! human! wastes!
(Tekerlekopoulou!et#al.,#2013).!Excessive!amounts!of!contaminant!compounds!can!have!a!
toxic! effect! on! humans! and! the! environment.! It! is! therefore! essential! to! remove! these!
compounds! from! groundwater! to! produce! drinking! water.! For! the! past! two! decades,!
research! has! focused! on! the! development! of! sustainable! technologies! for! the! removal! of!
NH4
+,!dissolved!iron!(Fe),!manganese!(Mn)!and!organic!carbon!in!groundwater.!At!present!
time,!biofiltration!is!one!of!the!most!commonly!used!methods!to!produce!drinking!water!
and! involves! microbial! degradation! processes! (Tekerlekopoulou! et# al.,! 2013).! While!
biofiltration! is! wellVknown! to! efficiently! remove! the! undesired! compounds,! the!
understanding! of! the! microbial! communities! in! these! filters! is! still! limited! and! requires!
further!investigation!to!optimize!drinking!water!treatment.!Moreover,!biofiltration!systems!
occasionally! fail! for! unknown! reasons! and! thus! understanding! the! microbial! communities!
could!help!to!mitigate!these!failures!and!optimize!operations.!

1.1.1 FILTRATION++
The! use! of! biological! sand! filters! for! water! purification! is! a! commonly! used! method! in!
Denmark!and!other!countries.!The!drivers!behind!include!the!minimised!chemicals!needed,!
cost! reductions! and! high! effectiveness.! There! exist! several! different! filter! technologies,!
hereunder!slow!and!rapid!sand!filters!(RSFs).!However,!due!to!their!greater!efficiency,!RSFs!
are!most!widespread!(Crittenden!et#al.,!2012).!!!!
The!purpose!of!RSFs!in!the!treatment!of!groundwater!is!to!remove!NH4
+,!Fe,!Mn!and!organic!
carbon! in! order! to! meet! the! regulatory! requirements! for! safe! drinking! water.! Regulatory!
standards!in!Europe!are!0.5!mg/L!for!NH4
+,!0.2!mg/L!for!Fe!and!0.05!mg/L!for!Mn!(European!
Commission,!2015).!However,!the!maximum!concentration!of!certain!compounds,!especially!
NH4
+,! is! significantly! stricter! (0.05! mg/L)! in! Denmark! (Miljøministeriet,! 2015).! This! is! to!
ensure! safety,! since! there! is! no! disinfection! step! with! chlorine! in! Danish! drinking! water!
treatment.!Table!2!shows!the!compounds!present!in!Danish!groundwater!and!each!of!their!
respective!limits!according!to!drinking!water!standards.!!
Removal! of! NH4
+,! Fe! and! Mn! occurs! physically,! chemically! and! biologically,! undergoing!
various!transformation!processes!that!are!described!further!in!the!following!section.!RSFs!

The! performance! of! RSFs! is! determined! by! the! ability! of! the! sand! filter! to! accumulate!
biomass! and! the! rate! of! substrate! utilization! of! microbes! (Søgaard! &! Madsen,! 2013).!
Microorganisms! are! very! sensitive! and! highly! dependent! on! environmental! conditions.!
Determining! factors! for! microbial! community! composition! include! pH,! temperature,!
dissolved! oxygen! (DO)! levels! and! the! nutrients! available! (Lytle! et# al.,! 2007;! Pacini! et# al.,!

essential! to! maintain! optimal! conditions! in! order! for! the! microbial! community! to! thrive.!!
Understanding!the!relationship!between!these!factors!and!nitrifying!microbes!is!therefore!

Contaminant! removal! in! RSFs! takes! place! through! physical,! chemical! and! biological!

2013)! and! the! microorganisms! carrying! out! these! processes! are! therefore! key! actors! in!
achieving!effective!removal!of!this!toxic!compound!(Albers!et#al.,!2015;!White!et#al.,!2012;!
Søgaard!&!Madsen,!2013).!The!present!thesis!addresses!more!specifically!the!nitrification!

1.2.1 AMMONIUM+TRANSFORMATION+
The!oxidation!of!NH4
+!to!nitrate!(NO3
V),!referred!to!as!nitrification,!is!a!core!process!in!RSFs.!
Nitrification!represents!the!main!nitrogen!transformation!in!RSFs!and!is!exclusively!carried!
out!by!distinct!microorganisms!(Madigan!et#al.,!2012;!Bernhard,!2010).!These!microbes!are!
chemolithotrophs,! meaning! that! they! obtain! their! energy! and! carbon! needed! for! cell!
synthesis!from!the!oxidation!of!nitrogen!compounds!(Madigan!et#al.,!2012;!Bernhard,!2010).!
Nitrification! occurs! aerobically! with! an! oxygen! demand! of! 3.6! mg/L! and! consists! of! two!
processes,!namely!nitritation!and!nitratation!(Zhu!et#al.,!2010).!!
Nitritation!is!carried!out!by!ammonium!oxidising!bacteria!(AOB)!and!ammonium!oxidising!
archaea! (AOA).! Nitritation! is! the! process! in! which! ammonia! (NH3)! is! oxidized! into! nitrite!
(NO2
V).! The! reaction! requires! two! key! enzymes.! Nitritation! is! initiated! by! the! membraneV
associated!enzyme!ammonia!monooxygenase!(AMO),!which!oxidizes!NH3!to!hydroxylamine!
(NH2OH).! NH2OH! is! subsequently! converted! to! nitrite! (NO2
V)! by! hydroxylamine!
oxidoreductase!(HAO).!The!remaining!electrons,!oxygen!and!free!hydrogen!then!assemble!to!
form! water! (Madigan! et# al.,! 2012;! Bernhard,! 2010;! Chain! et# al.,! 2003;! Bergmann! et# al.,!
2005).!Four!electrons!are!generated!through!nitritation.!However,!two!of!the!four!electrons!
are!shuttled!back!to!AMO!from!HAO!via!cytochrome!c,!i.e.!only!two!electrons!are!supplied!to!
the!terminal!oxidase!cytochrome!aa3!and!converted!to!energy!(Figure!2)!(Madigan!et#al.,!
2012;!Frijlink!et#al.,!1992).!!
!

1.4 QPCR!
Investigating! the! relationship! between! microbial! growth! and! the! rate! of! NH4

advanced!molecular!microbiological!techniques!can!be!applied!to!characterise!microbes.!In!
this! thesis! quantitative! polymerase! chain! reaction! (qPCR)! has! been! used! to! quantify! the!
most! abundant! taxa! in! RSFs.! qPCR! is! an! in# vitro! technique! used! to! amplify! and! detect! a!
specific!DNA!region!of!a!target!microorganism!from!various!environmental!samples!(Lim!et#
al.,!2011;!Zhang!&!Fang,!2006).!The!raison!d’être!of!the!qPCR!method!lies!in!its!ability!to!
quantitatively!analyse!target!organisms.!This!analysis!may!allow!for!the!estimation!of!growth!
in!the!target!organisms!over!time!and!enables!to!investigate!its!relationship!with!the!NH4
+!
removal!rate,!which!is!core!to!this!study.!!
Because!of!its!many!advantages!(Ziang!&!Fang,!2006;!Khot!&!Fredricks,!2009),!qPCR!is!one!of!
the!most!widely!used!methods!to!amplify!nucleic!acids.!Each!cycle!of!qPCR!consists!of!three!
steps:! denaturation,! annealing! and! extension# (Madigan! et# al.,! 2012).! In! the! denaturation!
step,!the!reaction!temperature!is!increased!and!the!complementary!doubleVstranded!DNA!is!
converted!to!singleVstrand!DNA.!The!melting!temperature,!Tm,!depends!on!the!concentration!
of!G/C!and!T/A!base!pairs!constituting!the!DNA!template!(Dieffenbach!et#al.,!1993).!In!the!
second!step,!the!temperature!is!decreased,!which!results!in!the!annealing!of!specific!primers!
to!each!end!of!the!target!singleVstranded!DNA!template.!In!the!third!step,!the!temperature!is!
raised!again,!resulting!in!an!extension!of!the!target!DNA!by!the!heatVstable!DNA!polymerase!
(Taq!DNA!polymerase)!adding!new!complementary!nucleotides,!dNTPs.!The!extension!time!
depends!on!the!length!of!the!target!sequence!–!the!longer!the!sequence,!the!longer!the!
extension! time! required.! After! each! cycle,! the! newly! synthesised! DNA! strands! can! act! as!
templates!in!the!next!cycle.!However,!the!lengths!of!the!products!of!a!first!and!second!cycle!
are!heterogeneous,!but!from!the!third!round!and!onwards,!the!DNA!with!defined!length!will!
increase!exponentially!(Kielberg!et#al.,!2003;!Madigan!et#al.,!212;!Lim!et#al.,#2011).!The!PCR!
products!are!detected!using!a!fluorescent!probe!such!as!SYBR!green,!which!binds!to!nonV
specific!dsDNA.!When!the!probe!binds!to!the!dsDNA,!it!emits!a!fluorescent!light!that!can!be!
detected!by!a!spectrofluorometer!after!every!realVtime!PCR!cycle.!All!data!are!collected!in!a!
software!program!that!calculates!the!number!of!gene!copies!based!on!the!standard!curve!
and!the!amplification!plot.!From!these!data,!it!is!possible!to!quantify!the!total!amount!of!
target!microbes!in!the!sand!sample.!!

of!amplification!highly!depends!on!the!quality!of!the!primers!(Dieffenbach!et#al.,!1993;!Lim!

1.4.1 DESIGN+OF+PRIMERS+FOR+QPCR+
Primers!consist!of!oligonucleotides!that!are!complementary!to!recognition!sites!of!the!target!
DNA.!The!purpose!of!primers!is!to!enable!replication!but!also!to!allow!qPCR!to!be!targetV
specific.!More!specifically,!primers!are!used!as!starting!point!for!DNA!replication!because!the!
enzyme,!DNA!polymerase,!cannot!begin!catalyzing!the!addition!of!new!nucleotides!without!a!
primer.!Selecting!primers!carefully!is!therefore!essential!to!ensure!that!only!the!target!DNA!
will! be! amplified! (specific)! and! the! DNA! product! increases! twofold! for! each! PCR! cycle!
(efficient)!(Dieffenbach!et#al.,!1993).!
There!are!several!criteria!for!designing!specific!and!efficient!primers:!primer!length,!melting!
temperature! (Tm),! GC! content,! selfVcomplementarity,! primerVdimer! formation! and! 3’end!
specificity!(Lim!et#al.,!2011).!!!
In! order! to! design! a! specific! primer! that! can! tolerate! a! high! annealing! temperature,! the!
primers! usually! have! a! length! of! 16V24! nucleotides.! There! is! also! a! direct! correlation!
between!primer!length!and!specificity!–!the!longer!the!primer,!the!higher!is!the!specificity!
for! the! target! DNA.! Each! time! the! primer! is! extended! by! one! nucleotide,! the! primer!
specificity!increases!fourfold.!However,!too!long!primers!should!be!avoided!as!it!can!lead!to!
a!decrease!in!the!overall!PCR!amplification!efficiency!(Lim!et#al.,!2011;!Dieffenbach!et#al.,!
1993).!!
The! primer! length! also! affects! the! melting! temperature,! Tm.! The! recommended! Tm! is!
between! 55oC! and! 60oC! and! is! decisive! for! establishing! the! annealing! temperature!
(Dieffenbach! et# al.,! 1993).! Tm! can! be! calculated! by! the! following! equation.! However,! it! is!

communities!in!RSFs.!

in!a!sand!sample!from!the!RSFs!and!was!constructed!by!cloning!the!DNA!of!interest.!The!
construction!of!an!accurate!standard!curve!is!hence!an!essential!prerequisite!for!ensuring!
precise!quantification!in!qPCR!assays.!
In!the!present!section,!the!results!from!the!qPCR!standards!are!presented,!followed!by!an!
analysis! and! discussion! of! the! constructed! standard! curves.! Finally,! the! efficiency! of! the!
qPCR!performance!is!evaluated!based!on!the!standard!curve.!!
Fresh!PCR!products!were!used!for!the!construction!of!standards!for!the!qPCR.!To!ensure!the!
quality,! the! PCR! products! were! loaded! on! agarose! gel.! As! before,! the! results! of! the! PCR!
should!give!single!bands!of!the!required!size!and!should!be!consistent!with!the!results!from!

qPCR!is!one!of!the!most!reliable!quantitative!methods!used!for!gene!expression!analysis.!
However,! the! results! of! qPCR! can! be! affected! by! many! experimental! variables! and! the!

made! in! triplicates.! Standard! deviations! of! the! triplicates! for! each! qPCR! reaction! were!

indication!of!how!close!the!copy!number!is!to!the!mean.!The!calculated!standard!deviations!
as!well!as!obtained!copy!numbers!are!listed!in!Table!14.!

The!benchNscale!RSF!experiment!investigated!the!relationship!between!microbial!growth!and!
NH4
+! removal! rate,! assessed! as! total! DNA! concentration! over! time.! The! column! run! five!

RSFs!due!to!the!high!standard!deviations!relative!to!the!obtained!copy!numbers!as!well!as!

DNA!from!the!sand!grains.!In!theory,!the!experimental!period!should!be!extended!to!20!days!

The! present! study! demonstrated! that! qPCR! is! a! highly! sensitive! and! accurate! method! for!
gene!expression!analysis.!Although!qPCR!in!this!study!was!unable!to!quantify!the!microbial!

6 PERSPECTIVES(
The! PCR! products! have! been! sent! for! pyrosequencing! in! order! to! provide! complete!
verification! of! each! of! the! designed! primer! pairs’! specificity.! These! results! are! still! under!
processing! and! are! therefore! not! included! in! the! present! study,! but! will! determine! the!
primers’!future!applicability.!Final!confirmation!of!primer!specificity!would!then!enable!the!
study! of! these! microorganisms! as! well! as! their! functions! in! a! wide! range! of! different!
environments.!
The! qPCR! assays! developed! in! the! present! study! did! not! enable! to! assess! the! growth! of!
microorganisms! in! relation! to! NH4
+! removal! in! RSFs.! However,! qPCR! is! one! of! the! most!
sensitive!and!powerful!methods!used!for!the!analysis!of!gene!expression.!The!study!could!
have! been! improved! by! repeating! the! DNA! extraction! from! the! sand! samples.! Moreover,!
extending! the! experimental! period! would! lead! to! increasing! microbial! growth! and! hence!
ensure!accurate!detection!of!copy!numbers.!
Reverse!transcription!polymerase!chain!reaction!(RTNPCR)!could!be!applied!in!extension!to!
the!present!study!in!order!to!gain!more!inNdepth!understanding!of!the!microbial!community!
in! RSFs.! Instead! of! DNA! based! detection,! RTNPCR! detects! RNA! expression.! While! DNA!
provides!information!about!the!microbes!carrying!the!target!gene,!detection!of!RNA!ensures!
exclusive!quantification!of!metabolically!active!cells.!Thus,!RTNPCR!provides!more!accurate!
information!than!qPCR!as!the!dead!cells!will!not!be!quantified.!Furthermore,!the!detection!
limit! for! RTNPCR! is! lower! than! qPCR.! However,! RNA! has! a! lower! stability! than! DNA! and!
extraction!of!RNA!can!be!therefore!challenging.!!!!!!!!!
Due! to! the! extent! of! the! present! study,! analysis! was! focused! on! bacteria! exclusively.!
However,!AOA!are!known!to!play!an!important!role!in!the!NH4
+!oxidation!process!in!RSFs!(de!
Vet!et#al.,!2009).!Including!archaea!in!future!studies!would!thus!be!interesting.!!
The! present! study! enables! to! gain! greater! insight! of! microbial! communities! and! their!
function!in!RSFs.!This!would!provide!tools!to!optimize!RSFs’!design!and!operation!in!drinking!
water!treatment!and!would!create!value!for!the!industry.!
