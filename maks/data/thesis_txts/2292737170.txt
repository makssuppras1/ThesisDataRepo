This master thesis is submitted as a partial fulfillment of the requirements for 
obtaining the Master of Science in Engineering degree in Sustainable Energy at

1.3. 
Chapter Outline 
 
The following outline provides an overview of the thesis in short, describing the 
content of each subsequent part. 
 
Chapter 2 will present the most current advancements that have been 
implemented in the area of energy savings in building renovations as well as the 
projects that will be used and compared with the results of this thesis 
 
Chapter 3 will discuss the different scenarios of high rate of replacement and 
deep renovation on the building mass as well as the procedure that will be 
followed, in their investigation throughout this project.  
 
Chapter 4 will present results from the different investigations and discuss 
observations. 
 
Chapter 5 will present a discussion on the study’s findings, the problems that 
arose throughout the duration of the project, as well as comparisons with 
current research and considerations for future investigations. 
 
Chapter 6 will conclude the present Thesis, summing up the observations 
throughout the course of this project and offering suggestions regarding future 
work and investment in the field of electret research.
