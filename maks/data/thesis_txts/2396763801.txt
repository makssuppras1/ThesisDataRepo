Abstract 
The purpose of this project is to develop an adjustable lampshade based on the study of current 
products and users. The survey has identified the needs of the users. A further analysis and 
research determined the final prototype of the proposed lampshade. 
Reconceptualization is the method applied to carry out further development of the final concept. 
After having performed numerous tests, the reconceptualized lampshade has been optimized to 
fulfill the needs of the users and other requirements from the project. 
The modular platform makes the lampshade more customizable and, hopefully more enjoyable.  
To determine the economic viability of the adjustable lampshade solution, a business plan has 
been developed, which includes the business model canvas tool, a market analysis and 
production budget.  
 
1.Project brief  
The scope of this master project is to create a re-conceptualized adjustable lampshade for 
home-use and along with this a suitable business model for the product, that has the most 
potential, both in needs and economic feasibility. Research on the target group, competitors and 
traditional products (lamps) will generate new ideas and designs that can be applied to the 
market as close as possible. A business case will be developed for the final concept in the end of 
the project. 
 
2.Project Objectives 
To get the best scope and have a base for evaluating the project, this project will address the 
following objectives: 
• Research different user groups, and create a business model that combines economic 
feasibility based on user studies and surveys. 
• Study light properties such as illumination, light source, refractions, interference.      
• Based on creative analysis and modelling, develop relevant models, systems and 
processes fit for user-needs  
• Using the KISS (Navy, 1960) rule, as method/Strategic to approach the new design. 
• Create mock-ups based on ideation results. 
• Detail product for a market launch.

In the everyday living space, people are more comfortable when brightness is around 50 to 80

Color temperature 
The color temperature is the temperature of an ideal black-body radiator that radiates light of a 
color comparable to that of the light source. In reality, the color temperature is meaningful for 
light sources that do in fact correspond somewhat closely to the radiation of some black body. 
Color temperature is scientifically expressed in Kelvin, using the symbol K. (Berns, 2000) 
Normally, Color temperatures over 5000 K are called "Cool colors", while lower color 
temperatures (2700–3000 K) are called "warm colors". "Warm" is an analogy to the radiated 
==PAGE:13==
 
12 
 
heat flux of traditional incandescent lighting rather than temperature. (Corporations, 2017) 
The spectral peak of warm-colored light is closer to infrared, and most natural warm-colored 
light sources emit significant infrared radiation. The fact that "warm" lighting in this sense 
actually has a "cooler" color temperature often leads to confusion. (DiLouie, 2016) 
For the lighting industry, it is often important to take into account the color temperature of 
illumination. A warmer light is often used in public and living areas to create relaxation, while a 
cooler light is used to enhance concentration and refreshment, for example in offices and 
schools. (Berns, 2000)

5.3 Market research (current) 
Market screening is a method used by companies to identify desirable markets and eliminates 
less desirable markets. Two different types of screening processes exist: country screening and 
segment screening (Li-Ying, 2017).  
During this project both processes were used, however, due to the large variations and different 
demographic characteristics, the segment screening was conducted in a form of a survey in 
order to narrow down some key indications relevant to this project. The overview of the country 
screening (market size) can be seen below. 
The Country screening uses whole countries as market selections. The country screening focuses 
on different market indicators to analyze the market in a country. Normally, a country screening 
would be performed in different countries to determine where is relevant to enter. (Li-Ying, 
2017)  
This project has focused on a few countries (US, China, Germany) to determine if there is a 
desirable market but concentrated mostly on countries which has the massive demographic 
dividend (Bloom, 1998) such as EU, US and China. The lampshade is a consumer product, 
therefore, most of the people have installed at their home.  
According to the study of the National Human Activity Pattern Survey (NHAPS), most people 
spend large amount of time at their residence, (Roberts, 2016) thus the number of newly built 
houses is a very good indicator for determining the market scale of this project.

6.6 Material selection  
Material selection is a very important step in the process of designing any physical product. The 
main goal of material selection is to balance the cost while meeting product performance goals. 
(Dieter, 1997) 
For this project, the top priority of this stage is selecting a suitable material for customizable 
Shades, because this component has the key value for the lampshade, and it is a very ingenious 
design compare with the existing products. Therefore, the material of the customizable shades 
must fulfill the following requirement: 
• Common  
• Flexible/Able to bend  
• Cheap  
• Easy to produce  
• Good appearance and environmental-friendly  
The EDU-PACK 2017 suggested that wood and polymer could be the best option for this project. 
It is inexpensive and lightweight material. The Yield strength shows it is an elastic material which 
means that it can bend and can be can cut easily. The hardness chart shows, the wood has bit 
lower hardness as the polymer materials (plastic), which means it is not a brittle material as 
glasses, thus it can be safely hung on the ceiling or walls. (Figure 36) 
 
Figure 36: Material selection: Hardness vs. Price 
==PAGE:64==
 
63 
 
According to the EDU-PACK 2017, the polymer and plywood have very similar properties in the 
categories of hardness, density and yield strength. To choose the optimum material for the 
project, a bending test was executed before running into the full-scale porotype. 
The project selected the Acrylic and plywood for the bending test. It made a 1:5 scale model to 
test the elasticity of the material and the bending mechanism of the shade. The result was very 
clear, the 3-mm acrylic is very hard to bend and it snaps at 90°. The 3-mm plywood is a flexible 
material, it can bend 180° without snap or cracks. (See Appendix: Bending test of acrylics p.24) 
And moreover, from the view of the light distribution, the plywood has rather a rough surface 
compared to the acrylic, meaning that it can a create great uniform diffusion instead of the 
refection caused by the smooth surface of the acrylic. From the aesthetics aspects, the wooden 
material makes people feel close the nature (Radkau, 2011).Moreover the wooden material 
considers the greenest choice of furniture industry (Dix, 2016). Thus, the plywood was selected 
to make the full-scale customizable shades.  
The material of remain components also is important. As the Handbook of visual communication 
addressed: 
A material that is homogeneous is uniform in composition or character (i.e. color, texture, 
design, etc.) came create a comfortable visual communication. (Smith, 2005) 
Therefore, the linkage hinges and framework bases should use the wooden material, such as 
plywood, wood sheet. For the light bulb cover, the best option is the translucent material, such 
as the semitransparent polymer. The adjusting wires should use the common nylon wire as the 
main material. For the retractable device, it should have wooden texture cover for the better 
unity appearance of the entire lampshade.

9.Further work  
The full-scale prototype meets the all the expectations and requirements after the test, but still 
there are some problems which need to be concerned about for the massive production.  
1. Manufacture tolerance 
During the cutting process the wood materials will have significant tolerance, normally it 
will be around ±0.2 mm. Under the large-scale production, the deviation may enlarge as 
well.  
2. Surface treatment 
Wood is very sensitive to the humidity and heat, therefore the surface treatment is 
critical for the durability of the product. To prevent the mold from cracking, the 
prototype used oil-coating as the primer surface treatment. It will be a good idea to coat 
a very thin layer of smooth paint or wax without effect the flexibility of the pattern hinge. 
Moreover, it should be easier to insert the wood into the connecting slot after the paint 
has been applied. 
3. Retractable device  
The retractable device uses the spring spiral as the main mechanism. It is modified from 
retractable plastic tamp measure. In the future this device can be integrated into the Z&H 
lamp platform with anti-jam system build in.               
4. The Framework set platform  
This project created 3 framework concepts for the platform, however due to the time 
limitation, only one functional prototype was made which is not enough for the entire 
product line. Thus in future, all those three framework concepts need to be build, test 
and validate thoroughly. 
5. The Profile Design Platform 
To make this platform succeed, it needs a large amount of designers contribute the 
contents to this community. To do this, a user-friendly website needs to be established. It 
should be accessed by the public, it should contain all the essential information about the 
product, platform, user guide, revenue plan.

10.Discussion  
As shown in the business case the product created by this project could be commercialized. In 
this project research and tests has been done throughout the entire process and a functional 
prototype for the adjustable lampshade has been developed. 
For this project, a lot of tests were performed based on the KISS method and selection tables. 
The IDEA 2 selected to a deep reconceptualization in the end. As a result, the Z&H lamp 6 has 
been built for proof of the concept. It covers many aspects such as User experience, adjusting 
unit, platform design and business approach.  
The functional Z&H lamp 6 (prototype)has given the project a very comprehensive view of the 
adjustable lampshade, as it has 6 detachable Profiles. The Profiles can be changed easily based 
on the taste of the users. To demonstrate the platform and modulization, the framework of Z&H 
lamp 4 was built. The linkage hinge and joint clip worked seamlessly between the different 
frameworks. However, due to the different framework proportion, the Z&H lamp 4 has its own 
Profiles, which is a bit smaller than the Z&H lamp 6 Profiles. 
The illumination of the lampshade will be different from the different Profiles. Users can adjust 
the brightness of the lamp by using the wire holder. The illumination efficiency of the Z&H lamp 
6 varies from 22.7% to 35.1%.

11.Conclusion  
Overall, the project has fulfilled the target requirement of the project scope. The details are 
listed below: 
This project made a process flowchart to find the optimum solution for the adjustable 
lampshade. A survey was used to tackle the user-needs. Furthermore, numbers of tests and 
selection tables have been used to ensure that the reconceptualized lampshade creates a better 
user experience than the current competitors. 
This project draws lessons from existing lampshade, identifying many possibilities for the new 
lampshade from Persona analysis and User journey map. Analyzing the survey and study the 
relevant field (Light properties) helped the project created 8 different ideas. KISS rule has applied 
to narrow it down to three ideas for further development. After thorough comparison of each 
idea, the IDEA 2 was chosen for further research and development. 
After going through the tests and improvement, the prototype of the lamp was made, and it was 
named as Z&H Lamp, along with the platform application and business model canvas. The 
prototype is a fully functional lampshade and the detachable profile is an elegant and ingenious 
solution for customization. The Profile design program allows external designers or third parties 
to join the platform and expand the variety of the Profiles to satisfy the users’ needs. 
The Z&H lamp is a totally different approach for adjustable lampshade. And it aims to create a 
community for the users across the world. It is a very innovative product compare with the 
traditional lampshade business. Nowadays, there has not been a similar product or service that 
could compete with the Z&H lamp. Therefore, it has a huge market potential in the lampshade 
industry.
