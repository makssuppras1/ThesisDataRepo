Abstract--
The!secondary!messenger!cMdiMAMP!is!a!signal!molecule,!which!is!involved!in!the!regulation!of!
several!important!mechanisms,!such!as!cell!wall!metabolism,!salt!homeostasis,!and!antibiotic!
resistance!in!gramMpositive!bacteria.!In!this!study!we!describe!the!correlation!between!high!
levls!of!cMdiMAMP!and!resistance!towards!butanol!and!hexanol!induced!stress.!We!conclude!a!
heat!resistant!and!salt!hypersensitive!phenotype!of!strains!adapted!to!high!concentrations!of!
butanol!and!hexanl.!However!we!are!not!able!to!replicate!the!same!phenotype!for!a!synthetic!
gdpP!deficient!strain!though!this!have!been!achieved!by!other!studies.!Microarray!analysis!
show!that!high!levels!of!cMdiMAMP!result!in!significant!up!regulation!of!gene!involved!with!cell!
wall!synthesis,!OMacetylation!of!the!peptide!glycan!layers,!as!well!as!DMalanine!substitution!in!
cell!wall!components.!It!is!furthermore!suggested!that!the!transcriptional!regulator!CodY!is!
inactivated!as!a!result!of!high!cMdiMAMP!concentration,!though!evidence!are!few.!It!is!also!
shown!in!this!study!that!high!cMdiMAMP!levels!can!be!achieved!putatively!by!increased!CdaA!
activity!which!leads!to!increased!(p)ppGpp!levels!and!thereby!to!GdpP!inhibition.!!

The-toxicity-profile-of-butanol-and-hexanol-
BioMalcohols!have!received!increasing!attention!during!the!last!15!years!due!to!their!relevance!
as!being!an!alternative!to!conventional!fuels.!Several!microorganisms!have!shown!great!
potential!as!productionMhosts!for!bioMalcohols!such!as!butanol!and!hexanol.!Escherichia$coli,$
Saccharomyces$cerevisia,!and!Basillus$subtilis!are!just!few!of!these!organisms,!however!the!
yield!of!bioMalcohol!produced!is!severely!limited!by!the!toxicity!of!the!alcohols.!This!is!due!to!
the!hydrophobic!properties!of!the!relatively!short!carbonMchain!found!in!these!alcohols,!which!
are!able!to!disrupt!the!phospholipid!components!of!the!cell!membrane!and!thereby!increase!
the!fluidity!of!them!membrane!and!destroy!the!integrity!of!the!cell!(1).!The!lactic!acid!bacteria!
(LAB)!Lactococcus$lactis!has!been!shown!to!be!fare!less!sensitive!to!high!alcohol!
concentrations,!as!well!as!being!a!suitable!production!organism!due!to!the!nature!of!its!
metabolism!and!the!large!array!of!genetic!tools!available!(1).!Other!than!what!is!described!
above,!very!little!is!known!about!how!L.!lactis!is!affected!by!high!concentrations!of!butanol!or!
hexanol.!Though!a!resent!but!unpublished!study!investigated!the!properties!of!ethanol,!
butanol,!and!hexanol!toxicity!and!found!butanol!to!be!cytotoxic!and!ethanol!and!hexanol!to!be!
cytostatic.!When!butanol!stress!was!applied!to!a!growing!culture,!growth!arrest!and!severe!
cell!death!was!observed.!Yet!butanol!does!not!lysis!the!cells,!as!the!optical!density!(OD)!did!
not!decrease.!Contrary!to!butanol,!almost!no!cell!death!occurred!when!ethanol!or!hexanol!
stress!was!applied,!though!growth!arrest!was!still!observed.!This!implies,!as!mentioned!above,!
that!butanol!stress!is!irreversible!and!therefore!cytotoxic,!where!as!the!effect!of!ethanol!and!
hexanol!stress!is!only!detectable!as!long!as!the!alcohols!are!present!and!can!therefore!be!
described!as!cytostatic.!Furthermore!was!the!cell!membrane!permeability!of!the!alcohol!
stressed!L.!lactis!cells!tested!by!observing!the!ability!of!orthoMnitroMphenylMgalactoside!
(ONPG)!to!cross!the!cell!membrane!in!a!betaMgalactosidase!assay.!The!permeabilization!
caused!be!the!three!alcohols!was!compared!to!the!normal!treatment!using!chloroform!and!it!
was!found!that!ethanol!had!little!to!no!effect,!while!treatment!with!butanol!and!hexanol!
resulted!permeability!close!to!that!of!chloroform.!This!strengthens!the!belief!that!damage!
caused!by!high!concentration!of!hexanol!is!revisable!but!still!causes!disruption!of!the!cell!
membrane!(1).!During!this!study!we!investigate!the!intercellular!response!of!butanol!and!
