==PAGE:3==
Abstract!!
Fires! occur! in! health! care! facilities! all! over! the! world.! In! Denmark!!
averagely! 10! fires! yearly! occur! in! hospitals! and! nursing! homes,! resulting!!
in!averagely!10!deaths!yearly!as!well.!!
The!current!theory!concerning!evacuation!and!calculation!thereof!assume!that!
all! occupants! have! the! same! physical! state! and! move! with! the! same! travel!
speed.!The!defined!travel!speeds!are!based!on!healthy!adults!without!any!kind!
of!disabilities.!Patients!at!a!hospital!cannot!be!assumed!to!move!with!the!same!
velocity!as!healthy!and!fit!people,!especially!not!bedridden!patients!who!are!in!
need!of!assistance!during!an!evacuation.!!
An!evacuation!experiment!is!planned!and!executed!concerning!evacuation!of!a!
bedridden! patient! within! a! hospital.! Two! evacuation! scenarios! are! studied.!
The! first! evacuation! scenario! concerns! evacuating! the! bedridden! patient!
horizontally!to!an!adjacent!fire!section,!whereas!the!second!scenario!concerns!
evacuating! the! bedridden! patient! 11! floors! down! the! emergency! staircase.!
Within! both! evacuation! scenarios,! the! evacuators! do! not! have! access! to!
additionally!assistance!devices,!besides!the!hospital!bed!in!which!the!patient!
are!lying!in.!!
==PAGE:4==
Abstract!
ii!
The!results!of!the!evacuation!experiments!include;!the!speed!of!a!hospital!bed!
and!a!mattress!with!a!patient!on!a!horizontal!surface,!the!turning!speed!of!a!
hospital!bed!being!turned!from!a!room!onto!a!corridor,!the!preparation!and!
transfer!time!of!two!male!porters!moving!a!mattress!including!patient!from!a!
hospital! bed! to! the! floor! and! the! descend! speed! of! evacuators! dragging! a!
mattress! including! patient! down! a! straight! staircase! including! platforms.!
Additionally,! measurements! of! the! physical! demand! of! the! male! evacuators!
performing!the!two!evacuation!scenarios!are!presented.!!
Based!on!the!results!obtained!through!the!evacuation!experiment,!it!can!be!
concluded! that! the! demand! of! a! male! evacuator! evacuating! a! bedridden!
patient!down!an!emergency!staircase!on!a!mattress!correspond!to!a!moderate!
work!out!for!a!common!person.!Furthermore,!it!is!concluded!that!the!current!
theory!concerning!fire!safety!design!is!inadequate!on!the!subject!of!evacuation!
of!bedridden!patients!concerning!the!calculation!of!the!required!safe!egress!
time.!

I!Danmark!opstår!der!gennemsnitligt!10!brande!årlige!indenfor!hospitaler!og!
plejehjem,!og!kræver!gennemsnitligt!10!liv!årligt.!!
Den! nuværende! evakueringsteori! og! beregning! af! denne,! antager! at! alle!
personer!der!opholder!sig!i!en!bygning!har!samme!fysik!og!bevæger!sig!med!
samme!hastighed.!Bevægelseshastigheden!af!en!person!er!baseret!på!voksne!
personer! fri! for! nogen! form! for! handicap.! Men! det! kan! ikke! antages! at!
patienter! indlagt! på! et! hospital! har! samme! bevægelseshastighed! eller! fysik!

af!assistance!i!tilfælde!af!evakuering.!!

2.1.1. Reaction!and!decision!time!
Once!a!fire!has!been!detected!and!a!warning!to!the!occupants!has!been!made,!
people!spend!time!on!reacting!to!the!alarm!and!time!on!deciding!what!to!do!or!
where! to! go.! Traditionally! it! was! assumed! that! all! occupants! would! start! to!
evacuate! as! soon! as! the! fire! alarm! sounded,! however! studies! and! inquiries!
have!been!made!that!contradict!this!assumption!(Proulx,!2002).!!
People!react!in!many!different!ways.!Some!people’s!first!action!is!to!find!and!
warn!other!people!or!children,!and!other’s!first!action!is!to!find!or!fight!the!
fire.! Only! approximately! 8! %! leaves! the! building! as! their! very! first! action!
(Bryan,! 2002).! The! human! behaviour! of! the! occupants,! and! thereby! their!
response! and! reaction! time,! is! also! dependent! on! the! usage! category! of! the!
building! and! the! type! of! warning! system.! The! British! Standards! Institute!
defined! and! suggested! delay! times! in! 1997,! which! are! based! on! a!
performance^based! approach! (Proulx,! 2002).! These! reaction! and! decision!
times!are!presented!in!Table!3,!where!it!is!noteworthy!that!a!warning!system!
with! a! live! voice! communication! results! in! a! vastly! shorter! decision! and!
reaction!time.!

3.3.1. Fire!safety!

general!assignments!is:!!
• Activate!the!emergency!plan!
• Notify! the! fire! department! and! the! emergency! coordination! centre!

• Fight!or!limit!the!fire!!
• Evacuate!the!departments/the!hospital!
• Handling!of!the!press!in!cooperation!with!the!region's!press!guard!
• Inform!the!patients!

5.!Conclusion!
An! evacuation! experiment! composed! of! two! different! evacuation! scenarios!
was!successfully!planned!and!executed!in!collaboration!with!Rigshospitalet!in!
Copenhagen.!The!porters!succeeded!in!evacuating!a!bedridden!patient!both!to!
an! adjacent! fire! section! on! the! same! level,! but! also! 11! floors! down! the!
emergency!staircase.!Both!evacuations!were!performed!without!the!need!of!
any!additional!aids!or!equipment,!than!the!bed!and!mattress!the!patient!was!
lying!on.!Based!on!the!data!extracted!from!the!evacuation!experiment!and!the!
results!presented!in!this!thesis,!it!is!possible!to!answer!the!research!questions!
stated!in!the!introduction.!!
The! results! of! the! experiment! concerning! evacuating! a! bedridden! patient!!
to! the! adjacent! fire! section! on! the! same! level! resulted! in! the! following!
conclusions;!1)!the!procedure!of!moving!the!patient!is!no!different!from!the!
daily! tasks! of! the! hospital! porters,! 2)! the! turning! speed! of! the! bed! when!
turning! from! the! room! onto! the! corridor! has! the! possibility! of! reaching! an!
average! speed! of! 0.94! [m/s]! depending! on! the! turning! method! used,! 3)! the!
average!speed!of!a!male!porter!pushing!a!hospital!bed!through!a!door!with!a!
free!width!of!1.28![m]!is!1.13![m/s]!and!4)!the!average!speed!of!a!male!porter!
pushing!a!hospital!bed!on!a!horizontal!surface!is!1.34![m/s].!!

Based! on! the! results! and! the! analysis! of! these,! it! can! be! concluded! that! the!
current! theory! concerning! fire! safety! design! does! not! incorporate! the!
evacuation!of!bedridden!patients,!or!any!other!kind!of!disabled!occupants.!The!
evacuation! theory! is! therefore! inadequate! when! it! comes! to! predicting! the!

of!the!practical!evacuation!experiment.!!

etager. 
 
Du har ved mail af dd spurgt, om ovennævnte projekt skal anmeldes til det videnskabsetiske komite-
system.  
 
Jeg har vurderet, at der ikke er tale om et sundhedsvidenskabeligt forskningsprojekt som dette er de-
fineret i komitélovens § 2,1.  
 
Projektet er derfor ikke anmeldelsespligtigt, jf. komitélovens § 1, stk. 4 og kan iværksættes uden til-
ladelse fra De Videnskabsetiske Komiteer for Region Hovedstaden. 
 
I Danmark har det videnskabsetiske komitesystem til opgave at vurdere sundhedsvidenskabelige 
forskningsprojekter.  
 
Ved et sundhedsvidenskabeligt forskningsprojekt forstås projekter, der indebærer forsøg på levende-
fødte menneskelige individer, menneskelige kønsceller, der agtes anvendt til befrugtning, menneske-
lige befrugtede æg, fosteranlæg og fostre, væv, celler og arvebestanddele fra mennesker, fostre og 
lign. eller afdøde. Herunder omfattes kliniske forsøg med lægemidler på mennesker og klinisk af-
prøvning af medicinsk udstyr.  
  
Sundhedsvidenskabelig forskning omhandler primært forskning inden for de lægevidenskabelige fag, 
den kliniske og den socialmedicinsk-epidemiologiske forskning. Begrebet omfatter, udover forskning 
af de somatiske sygdomme, tillige de psykiatriske og de klinisk-psykologiske sygdomme og til-
standsformer. Herudover inddrages tilsvarende odontologisk og farmaceutisk forskning under begre-
bet. 
 
 
Der ligger således ikke i afvisningen af at bedømme projektet nogen etisk stillingtagen eller negativ 
vurdering af dets indhold.

Klagevejledning: 
 
Komitéens afgørelse kan, jf. komitélovens § 26, stk. 1, indbringes for Den Nationale Videnskabseti-
ske Komité, senest 30 dage efter afgørelsen er modtaget. Den Nationale Videnskabsetiske Komité 
kan, af hensyn til sikring af forsøgspersonernes rettigheder, behandle elementer af projektet, som ikke 
er omfattet af selve klagen.  
Klagen skal indbringes elektronisk og ved brug af digital signatur og kryptering, hvis protokollen in-
deholder fortrolige oplysninger.  
Dette kan ske på adressen: dketik@dketik.dk  
Klagen skal begrundes og være vedlagt kopi af Den Regionale Videnskabsetiske Komités afgørelse 
samt de sagsakter, som Den Regionale Videnskabsetiske Komité har truffet afgørelse på grundlag af.
