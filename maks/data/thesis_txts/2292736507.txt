Abstract!
!
This!master’s!thesis!explores!the!role!and!potential!of!Design!for!Manufacturing!and!Assembly!
(DFMA)!in!reducing!engineering!changes!and!improving!new!product!development!at!Grundfos!
A/S.!It!is!assumed!that!engineering!changes!cause!a!delay!in!time!to!profit.!This!thesis!seeks!a!
solution!to!reduce!time!to!profit.!
!
This!thesis!begins!with!a!review!of!the!literature!in!the!field!of!DFMA,!concurrent!engineering!
and!product!development.!It!identifies!how!DFMA!activities!are!or!are!supposed!to!be!included!
in!the!product!development!process.!It!also!provides!an!overview!of!engineering!change!
management.!
!
The!initial!hypothesis!of!the!case!study!was!that!insufficient!knowledge!or!use!of!DFMA!was!a!
major!contributor!to!the!large!number!of!late!engineering!changes!at!Grundfos.!An!analysis!is!
presented!that!consists!of!a!quantitative!and!qualitative!review!of!engineering!change!notes!and!
interviews!with!product!development!stakeholders.!It!was!found!that!the!problem!was!due!to!
lack!of!communication,!project!structure!and!follow!up!procedures.!DFMA!knowledge,!methods!
and!information!are!available!at!Grundfos,!but!their!use!is!insufficient.!
!
To!facilitate!the!use!of!DFMA!in!industry,!this!work!proposes!extending!DFMA!with!a!framework!
that!includes!company!specific!production!capabilities,!in!addition!to!generic!guidelines.!It!
acknowledges!that!DFMA!should!be!included!in!the!early!design!phase!and!proposes!ways!to!do!
so.!I!argue!that!manufacturing!be!taken!into!consideration!during!the!process!of!identifying!
constraints.!Because!the!final!design!constraints!depend!on!the!chosen!shape!and!material,!the!
main!manufacturing!process(es),!and!the!manufacturing!process!chain,!this!cannot!be!done!in!a!
single!iteration.!To!illustrate!how!these!decisions!are!linked!and!how!the!process!chain!can!be!
included!when!the!initial!decisions!about!shape,!material!and!the!primary!manufacturing!
process!are!made,!I!propose!the!Cycle!of!Constraints.!I!also!argue!that!process!chains!should!be!
considered!as!part!of!concept!generation!and!selection!and!present!an!extended!model!of!Ulrich!
and!Eppinger’s!Product!Development!Process!to!illustrate!how!this!can!be!done.!!
!
Finally,!this!work!provides!suggestions!to!reduce!the!number!of!engineering!changes!and!
improve!time!to!profit!at!Grundfos.!I!propose!initiating!a!production!capability!loop!that!ensures!
that!Grundfos!capabilities!are!actively!used!from!the!early!design!phase.!I!propose!that!product!
quality!key!performance!indicators!(KPI)!be!established!as!a!tool!to!measure!and!control!DFMA!
parameters!(tolerance,!material,!Design!for!Manufacturing,!and!Design!for!Assembly)!along!the!
development!process.!The!proposed!quality!KPIs!are!based!on!a!scoring!of!the!design!
compatibility!with!Grundfos!capabilities.!It!is!expected!that!KPIs!and!the!proposed!capability!
lists!will!be!central!tools!to!fulfill!the!capability!loop!and!support!concurrent!engineering.!

Chapter!1 
Introduction!
!
For!decades,!Grundfos!has!been!recognized!for!the!design!and!production!of!high!quality!and!
advanced!technology!pumps.!Historically,!competitors!have!been!able!to!copy!their!designs!
within!a!few!years!after!market!launch.!Therefore,!their!development!process!has!focused!more!
on!quality!than!time!to!market.!However,!new!EU!regulations!have!restricted!the!sale!of!low!
energy!efficiency!pumps,!forcing!Grundfos!to!develop!pumps!with!higher!energy!efficiency!
within!a!fixed!and!reduced!time!frame.!From!the!1st!of!January!2013,!an!Energy!using!Products!
Directive!(EuP),!required!an!energy!efficiency!index!(EEI)!of!no!more!than!0,27.!This!means!that!
pumps!with!energy!labels!below!A!are!not!allowed!to!be!sold!in!Europe.!!!
!
Two!of!the!projects!started!to!fulfill!this!requirement!were!SP!and!RW.!Both!projects!had!high!
costs!and!delayed!time!to!market.!The!projects!also!required!a!large!number!of!engineering!
changes!after!the!final!designs!were!frozen.!
!
In!the!literature,!late!design!changes!are!associated!with!large!expenses,!including!sourcing!and!
scrap,!interruptions!in!production!flow,!rework,!and!delays!(Wright!1997).!For!example,!
Terwiesch!and!Loch!(1999)!reported!that!one!third!to!one!half!of!the!total!engineering!capacity!
is!consumed!by!engineering!changes,!and!20e50%!of!total!tool!costs.!Grundfos!has!also!
experienced!high!development!costs!and!delayed!time!to!market!as!a!result!of!their!engineering!
changes.!!
!
Grundfos!seeks!to!identify!the!causes!of!the!many!engineering!changes!and!opportunities!for!
improvements.!
!
This!project!was!initiated!by!the!Quality$&$Environments!department!at!Grundfos!A/S.!Their!
responsibility!is!quality!in!production!and!they!are!highly!influenced!by!the!many!changes!of!
design!after!the!product!has!entered!production.!Their!motivation!is!to!design!quality!into!the!
product.!
!

(missing)!elements!of!DFMA!that!contributed!to!the!most!changes!and!to!propose!a!framework!
to!improve!Design!for!Manufacturing!and!Assembly!in!product!development.!

Chapter!2 
Prior!Art!
!
Historically,!the!design!and!manufacture!of!products!was!seen!as!a!sequence!of!isolated!activities!
in!which!“the!tasks!of!the!upstream!phase!were!completed!before!tasks!for!a!downstream!phase!
were!started”!(Prasad,!1995).!Projects!passed!through!each!department,!from!marketing!
through!to!production!and!sales,!without!attention!to!collaboration.!This!approach!is!referred!to!
as!sequential!engineering,!or!“overetheewall”!product!development!(Figure!1)!(Reid!and!Sanders,!
2010).!!
!
!
Figure!1!Sequential!vs.!Concurrent!design.!Reprinted!(Reid!and!Sanders,!2010)!
In!first!half!of!the!19th!century,!research!and!industry!began!to!recognize!the!importance!of!
multiefunctional!teams!during!the!product!development!phase!(Smith,!1997).!This!led!to!the!
development!of!concurrent!engineering!(CE)!(Figure!2).!!
!
!
Figure!2!Concurrent!Engineering!(Sohlenius!2008)!
!
A!key!element!of!concurrent!engineering!is!the!inclusion!of!manufacturing!considerations!and!
tasks!in!the!design!phase.!Design!for!Manufacturing!and!Assembly!(DFMA)!is!a!set!of!guidelines!
and!tools,!which!can!be!used!in!concurrent!engineering!to!improve!and!support!collaboration!

2.1 !Design!for!Manufacturing!and!Assembly!!
!
Research!has!shown!that!around!80%!of!manufacturing!costs!are!determined!by!the!design!of!
the!product!(O’Driscoll,!2001;!Anderson,!2014).!Careful!consideration!to!production!processes!
during!the!design!phase!can!lead!to!higher!quality!products!at!a!lower!cost!and!with!a!faster!time!
to!market!(Boothroyd!et!al.,!2002).!
!
Design!for!Manufacturing!and!Assembly!is!a!set!of!guidelines!and!tools!to!optimize!designs!for!
ease!of!manufacture!and!assembly!(Bralla,!1999;!Boothroyd!et!al.,!2002;!Anderson,!2014).!Cost!
and!time!are!used!as!measures!for!optimization;!various!methods!are!presented!to!estimate!and!
benchmark!cost!(Bralla,!1999;!Boothroyd!et!al.,!2002;!Ulrich!and!Eppinger,!2011;!Anderson,!
2014).!!

Engineering!Changes!
2.3.1
!
An!engineering!change!(EC)!is!defined!by!Wright!(1997)!as!a!modification!to!a!component!of!a!
product,!after!that!product!has!entered!production.!EC!is!often!seen!as!a!manufacturing!issue!
(Martel,!1988;!Reldelbach,!1991).!A!general!EC!process!presented!by!Tavcara!and!Duhovnik!
(2005)!is!shown!in!Figure!11.!
!
!
!
Figure!11!EC!Process.!Reprinted!from!Tavcara!and!Duhovnik,!2005!
!
ECs!have!considerable!cost!implications!(Wright,!1997)!and!are!associated!with!large!expenses,!
including!sourcing!and!scrap,!interruptions!in!production!flow,!rework,!and!delays!(Wright!
1997).!The!impact!of!ECs!is!larger!for!products!with!long!lead!times!(Reidelbach,!1991)!because!
each!change!is!more!likely!to!have!to!be!incorporated!into!products!that!are!already!on!the!shop!
floor!(Wright,!1997).!The!impact!of!engineering!change!is!also!dependent!on!the!production!
investment.!!
!
Prasad!(1996)!states!that!once!a!product!is!in!production,!the!product!excellence!is!decreasing,!
as!competitors!and!markets!are!evolving.!This!means!that!ECs!are!not!only!expensive!internally!
(due!to!rework,!delays!etc.),!they!can!also!be!expensive!in!terms!of!lost!opportunity!in!the!
external!market.!!

Engineering!Change!Management!
2.3.2
!
Engineering!change!management!is!the!discipline!of!managing!product!and!process!related!

prepared!for!changes.!Historically!EC!research!has!been!carried!out!from!a!manufacturing!
perspective,!which!ignores!its!capacity!to!provide!the!engine!for!product!improvement,!and!

3.1 !Introduction!to!Grundfos!
!
Grundfos!A/S!is!one!of!the!world’s!largest!manufacturers!of!pumps!(Grundfos.com,!2015a).!!They!
serve!a!wide!range!of!customers!all!over!the!world!in!industries!such!as!agriculture,!water!
treatment,!mining,!commercial!buildings,!and!food!(Grundfos.com,!2015c).!Their!annual!
production!is!more!than!16!million!pumps,!with!a!yearly!turnover!in!2013!of!3.118!mil.!Euros!
(Grundfos.com,!2015b)!
!
Pump!manufacturers!usually!produce!a!large!product!portfolio!to!serve!specific!customers!
requirements.!For!example,!customers!may!need!specific!product!configurations,!depending!on!
the!flow,!pressure,!and!capacity!they!require.!Within!one!product,!hundreds!of!variants!are!
developed.!This!ensures!the!most!energy!efficient!pump!for!the!each!application.!!
!
The!recent!product!development!projects!at!Grundfos!have!been!motivated!by!new!EU!
regulations!(Grundfos,!2010b),!and!the!requirements!for!product!development!have!changed.!
Grundfos!is!a!market!leader,!and!has!for!years!been!ahead!of!its!competitors.!It!is!estimated!that!
it!usually!takes!3!years!for!competitors!to!introduce!a!competing!product.!It!has!therefore!been!
more!important!to!be!right!the!first!time!rather!than!have!a!fast!product!launch.!However,!the!
deadlines!associated!with!new!EU!regulations!are!now!forcing!projects!to!be!developed!with!
strict!deadlines.!Highespeed!development!is!now!an!important!issue!at!Grundfos.!Design!for!
Manufacturing!and!Assembly!is!expected!to!help!reduce!time!to!profit.!
!
In!addition,!until!recently!Grundfos!has!not!been!very!focused!on!cost!reduction!activities.!This!

5.1.2.1 
The!Process!of!Identifying!the!Drivers!of!Constraints!
In!a!product!design!project!shape,$material!and!processes!are!all!important!decisions!for!the!designers!
and!engineers!to!make.!These!three!elements!of!a!design!are!highly!interlinked,!and!make!the!
decisions!complex!(Ishii,!Lee,!and!Miller,!1990).!
!
The!performance!of!any!manufacturing!process!is!a!result!of!the$shapeTmaterialTprocess$combination,!
decisions!made!by!the!designers!and!engineers.!The!combinational!relationships!have!many!
influences,!e.g!MaterialTshape!determines!loads!and!material!response,!ShapeTprocess!is!often!
associated!with!tradeoff!between!processing!time!and!complexity!vs.!part!count!(Lovatt!and!Shercliff,!
1998a).!
!
In!product!design,!the!rough!shape!is!often!decided!upon!first.!The!sequence!of!process!and!material!
selection!depends!on!the!design!motivation!(performanceedriven!or!costedriven)!(Lovatt!and!
Shercliff,!1998a).!A!common!approach!is!to!select!the!material!first,!as!suggested!by!Pahl!and!Beitz!
(2005)!and!Ashby!(2010).!The!objective!is!to!balance!the!decision!to!find!a!feasible!shapeTmaterialT
process!combination,!which!performs!well!on!the!four!factors!(rate,!cost,!time!and!quality)!(Mital!et!
al.,!2008).!!
!
To!do!DFMA!properly,!a!minimum!geometry,!material,!and!process!have!to!be!chosen.!These!three!
things!do!not!have!to!be!decided!in!a!specific!order!and!manufacturing!considerations!should!
influence!those!decisions!regardless!of!what!order!they!do!in.!
!
The!main!process!is!often!what!is!given!the!most!attention.!We!have!seen!this!in!DFMA!where!a!set!of!
process!specific!guidelines!are!applied!to!the!component,!without!attention!to!upstream!or!
downstream!processes.!But!these!secondary!processes!can!be!a!major!contributor!to!production!
costs.!This!was!also!seen!at!Grundfos,!“Focus$is$often$on$the$primary$process,$secondary$processes$are$
neglected”$(stainless!steel!production!engineer).!If!the!process!chain!constraints!are!not!considered!
until!late!in!the!process!as!suggested!by!Cross’!(1989),!French’s!(1999),!Pahl!and!Beitz’s!(2005),!and!
Ulrich!and!Eppinger’s!(2011),!this!may!impose!changes!where!it!is!often!too!expensive!to!change!the!
decision.!This!emphasizes!the!importance!of!considering!the!process!chain!in!the!requirements!

Chapter!6 
!Conclusion!
!
Design!for!Manufacturing!(DFM)!is!currently!presented!as!generic!guidelines!for!component!
optimization!after!the!shape,!material!and!process!has!been!defined,!together!with!Design!for!
Assembly!(DFA)!activities!to!optimize!the!complete!design.!!
!
The!hypothesis!of!the!case!study!was!that!insufficient!knowledge!or!use!of!DFMA!is!a!major!
contributor!to!the!large!number!of!late!engineering!change!notes!at!Grundfos.!The!analysis!of!the!case!
study!showed!that!the!two!largest!contributors!to!the!large!number!of!change!notes!were!the!
organization!of!information!and!resources!in!the!projects!and!the!compatibility!of!the!design!with!the!
production!capabilities.!
!
The!aim!of!this!work!was!to!investigate!DFMA!in!an!industrial!context!and!to!determine!if!it!can!be!
used!to!reduce!late!engineering!changes!and!as!a!result!reduce!time!to!profit.!The!intention!of!the!
DFMA!guidelines!is!to!help!the!designer!to!be!“right!the!first!time”!and!optimize!the!design!to!improve!
ease!of!manufacturing,!time!and!cost.!Current!DFMA!guidelines!are!based!on!production!
characteristics.!
!
At!Grundfos!I!found!more!than!1300!engineering!change!notes!in!the!RW!project.!More!than!65%!of!
these!are!related!to!manufacturing!and!assembly!capabilities.!The!conclusion!was!that!the!current!
DFMA!activities!are!insufficient!to!incorporate!the!production!capabilities,!and!reduce!the!number!of!
changes!notes.!
!
A!number!of!concepts!have!been!identified!at!Grundfos!to!support!DFMA.!These!activities!have!not!
been!identified!in!the!literature.!The!framework!and!implementation!of!these!quality!tools,!methods!
and!guidelines!are!insufficient,!and!there!is!no!follow!up!on!these!activities.!This!means!that!they!are!
often!given!low!priority!in!projects!under!time!pressure.!!
!
The!background!for!Grundfos!to!implement!DFM!was!that!Grundfos!has!recognize!that!changes!after!
design!freeze!(DP4),!and!later!(DP5/6)!lead!to!activities!that!cause!problems!related!to!quality!and!
delivery.!The!reason!for!this!is!said!to!be!lack!of!knowledge!of!process!tolerance!capability,!and!that!
process!tolerance!capabilities!are!not!systematically!incorporated!in!construction!of!products.!For!
example!it!was!said!that!“All$tolerances$in$the$SP$project$have$doubled”.!The!findings!of!the!case!study!
support!the!view!that!they!are!not!designing!for!production!capabilities.!But!based!on!the!literature!
review,!it!is!concluded!that!DFMA!is!not!a!sufficient!solution.!
!
